# Portal References

This folder stores local references to the Jira customer portal.

It is not a mirror of the portal and does not contain full exported knowledge-base articles.

What is stored here:

- The canonical portal URL.
- Ticket-derived snippets that mention the customer portal or knowledge-base resources.

What is not stored here:

- Full portal pages.
- Attachments or article bodies fetched directly from the customer portal.

If a full local copy is needed later, that should be done as a separate authenticated export or scrape workflow.
