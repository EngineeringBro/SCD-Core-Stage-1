Printing the header and footer of a page is determined by your browser settings. If you want to remove the header and footer and only print the ticket content, you will need to remove them on your print page settings.

Google Chrome

When viewing the print dialog box, uncheck the option for Headers and Footers.

Firefox

1. Click the Options button on the right side of the page.

2. From the list of options, click Print.

3. On the Print Settings page, scroll down and uncheck the box next to Print Headers and Footers.

Safari

1. Print the ticket using the Browser Dialog option.

2. In the print dialog box, uncheck the box next to Print Headers and Footers.

Microsoft Edge

1. Click the Options button on the right side of the page.

2. From the list of options, click Print.

3. On the Print Settings page scroll down and expand the More Settings section. Uncheck the box next to Headers and Footers.
