There are several reasons why you might need to update your staff Username or PIN/password for logging into RepairQ. Whether you've forgotten your credentials, need to enhance security, or are simply updating account details, our software makes the process straightforward.

Users with the Manager role or higher can assist in updating these credentials directly within RepairQ. Alternatively, staff members can use the Password Reset option on the login screen to securely update their password without assistance.

Change Your Username and Password

A user with an Admin or Manager role will need to log into RepairQ. On the Navigation Bar, go to Staff → View Staff. On the Staff Overview page, locate the user that needs changes made. Under the Actions column, click Edit.

Note: If you are working with a large number of users, there are filters on the left column of the Staff Overview page to locate a specific Staff Member.

Scroll down to the Login Information section and edit/enter the new Username and/or PIN. Make sure any other required fields are filled out. Once you are satisfied with your changes, click the Save button to update your settings.

You can view any changes made to a User Profile by viewing the Change Log at the bottom of the Staff Management page.

Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your PIN/Password, you can reset your password via an email link.

Open your web browser and navigate to your store's URL. Your RepairQ URL will be similar to https:businessname.repairq.io or 401b-ca1001.repairq.io. On the login screen, click the "Forgot Password?" link next to the Login button.

Enter the email address associated with your Staff Profile and click Recover. You should receive an email with a link to reset your password within a few minutes. If you don't see the email, be sure to check your spam folder. Follow the link and establish a new PIN/Password. Your PIN can be numeric or alphanumeric, based on your preference. After you have established a new PIN, you can log into RepairQ.
