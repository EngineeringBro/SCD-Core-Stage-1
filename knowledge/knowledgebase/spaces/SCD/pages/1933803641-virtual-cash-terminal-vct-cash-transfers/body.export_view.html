There may be situations where you need to transfer cash from a Virtual Cash Terminal (VCT), such as moving cash to a safe or making payouts to reimburse for services or supplies. Users with a Shift Lead, Manager, or higher role can perform these tasks, including transferring cash between VCTs or pulling cash from a VCT.

Perform a Cash Transfer

Log into a computer with an assigned VCT. On the Navigation Bar, click the Location Short Name to expand a list of options. On the dropdown, click Cash Transfer.

On the Cash Transfer pop-up, fill out the necessary information including whether you are adding or removing funds from the VCT, the Amount, the Destination or Source, and any notes for the transfer. Click Transfer Cash to confirm the transfer. A confirmation alert will notify you of the new cash balance of the VCT.

  • Add/Remove - Select whether you are adding cash to this VCT or removing cash from this VCT.

  • Amount - Enter the amount that will be added to the VCT or subtracted from the VCT.

  • From Source/To Destination - Select the Source when adding cash to the VCT or the Destination if removing cash from the VCT.

    • Open VCT - Cash can only be transferred between VCTs when multiple terminals are assigned.

    • Not a VCT - Use when transferring to an outside source (Safe, Bank Deposit, etc.).

  • Notes - Add any additional notes to help log details about the transfer.

Reconciliation Report Cash Transfers

When closing your VCT, any transfers will be recorded in the Transfers tab of the Reconciliation Report.
