Release 07/16/2018 11:45PM CT (16:45 UTC)

Improvements

We have tightened the default invoice view and remittance permissions. Now the permissions to view, edit, or record remittance on an invoice will be restricted to the invoice's "created from" location.

If this update is too restrictive for your business practices, we recommend granting your affected users the "Invoicing" role at each of the locations you wish them to manage invoices for.
