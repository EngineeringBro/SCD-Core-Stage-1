This is a minor update to address bugs and add minor features.

Backordered items - Automated linking

When a purchase order is advanced to the "Ordered" status, any backordered items that can be fulfilled are linked up to the PO and advanced from "backordered" to "ordered." When reverting a purchase order from the ordered status back to the new/draft, and then forward again to the ordered status it could link up additional backordered items beyond the ordered qty resulting in more items linked to a purchase order than could be fulfilled by that purchase order. This issue has been fixed. In addition, RepairQ will prevent reverting a purchase order from the "ordered" status back to "new/draft" if any inventory items are already linked up to the order.
