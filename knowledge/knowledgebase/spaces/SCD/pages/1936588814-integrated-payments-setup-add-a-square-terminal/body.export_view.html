Signing out of your Square Terminal

If using your Square Terminal for non-integrated payments, you must sign out of your Square device before integrating with RepairQ.

From the left bezel of your Square Terminal screen, swipe right to bring up Options. Then tap Settings. Next, Tap Sign out at the bottom of the Settings screen.

Add a Square Terminal to RepairQ

Log into the RepairQ location that will use this terminal. Navigate to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings Overview page, click on the Payments dropdown on the left column. From the list of options, click Payment Devices.

On the Payment Terminal page, click Add a Payment Device on the right side of the screen and begin filling out the available fields. Once you are satisfied with your selections, click Save.

  • Location - This field should be assigned to where you are logged in.

  • Name - Give your payment device a recognizable name when assigning it to a VCT.

  • Payment Method - Select the Payment Method that uses the Square gateway.

  • Hardware - Select Square Terminal.

  • Skip customer receipt preference - Select whether or not to skip the option to ask a customer for their receipt preference on the Square Terminal during the transaction process.

After saving your settings, a Device Pairing pop-up window with a code to enter on your Square Terminal will appear.

Pairing the Square Terminal with RepairQ

Once you see the paring code in RepairQ, tap Sign In on the Square device. Then, on the sign-in screen, tap Use a device code. This will allow you to input the code that was generated from RepairQ. After entering the code, tap Sign In on the top-right corner of the screen to complete the pairing process.

Note: Do not enter your email and password on the sign-in screen. RepairQ can integrate only through the device pairing code issued from the application.

Once you have paired your device, click Test Pairing to confirm that RepairQ is connected to your terminal. If the device was successful in pairing, you will see a Pairing Successful message, and you can close the window.
