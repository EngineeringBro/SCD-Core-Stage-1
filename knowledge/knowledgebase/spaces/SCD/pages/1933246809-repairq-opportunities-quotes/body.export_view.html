RepairQ offers opportunity management for contacts (leads), customers, opportunities (quotes), and appointments. Users can easily create quotes that can be printed, emailed, and converted into tickets.

A quote has all of the features of a standard ticket with a couple of key differences:

  • Inventory is not pulled nor consumed for a quote.

  • Quotes are not included in revenue reports.

Quotes also use the same status system that you use for tickets and repairs. Converting a quote into an active ticket is a one-click process, and once converted, the related inventory is immediately pulled to the ticket.

Customer Quote Tools in RepairQ

Users can locate Quote features by going to the Opportunities section of the Navigation Bar.

  • + Contact - Create a new contact. This is a potential customer, i.e. lead, for your business. They are listed separately from your Customer list. They can be changed to a customer in their profile settings or by converting a quote they are assigned into a ticket.

  • View Contacts - View your full list of contacts and customers. This list can also be sorted to show only leads.

  • View Quotes - View a list of all quotes in your ticket queue.

  • Appointments - Create and manage appointments for customers and leads.

Identify a Quote in your Ticket Queue

Quotes have an assigned number, just like a standard ticket. When searching your ticket queue, you can search for a ticket number or filter for Type: Quote. The Ticket Type (TT) indicator for a quote is a quotation mark symbol.

When viewing a ticket, a quote is identified by the ticket type on the upper-left hand of the page.

Save a Ticket as a Quote

If you create a new ticket and would like to save it as a quote for your customer, you must have a customer and a repair device, item, or labor attached to the ticket. If the ticket meets these requirements, a new selection will appear at the bottom of the ticket, along with the standard ticket save statuses. Click Save as Quote to convert the ticket to a quote, which will be searchable in your Quote List and Ticket List (if filtered to include quotes).

Note: This option will only be possible when saving the ticket for the first time. If it has already been progressed to an existing status, this option will not be available.
