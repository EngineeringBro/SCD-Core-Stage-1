Integrated Payment systems give users a secure solution for accepting payments on a payment terminal and seamlessly posting those transactions to a RepairQ ticket. This streamlines the checkout process and reduces accounting errors when compared to manually recording payments on tickets. Bluefin is currently available for customers in the United States, and Square is available in the United States and Canada. Their devices support Europay, MasterCard, Visa (EMV), and Point-to-Point Encryption (P2PE) payment processing for secure and reliable payment processing.

After setting up your account with Bluefin and receiving your device, the first step is to activate the device via the Bluefin P2PE Manager. If you have any questions or concerns please call Bluefin Technical Support at 1-800-675-6573.

Activating your Bluefin Device

There are 4 general steps that must be completed before your device is active and ready to use. An additional document with detailed steps about the inspection and activation process will be provided by Bluefin. Note: Please do not open the tamper-sealed bag or throw away any packaging until the end of activation. This is to ensure proper security and setup for the device. The tamper seal will have the information required for the activation process.

Logging In to the P2PE Manager

The P2PE manager is where you will control the state of your devices. You can access the P2PE Portal by going to https://bluefin.p2pemanager.com. Prior to your device shipment, the Custodian (your company’s P2PE point person) of the P2PE device will have received a login to the P2PE Manager via email from Bluefin. Look for an email with a subject similar to: P2PE POI Manager: Account Created.

On the P2PE Portal login page, enter your User Name and Password provided in the email mentioned above.

Change Your P2PE Portal Password

Once you log in to the portal, we recommend you change your password for security purposes. Inside the "change password" area, you will be able to review your information and make any updates necessary as well as change your password. Follow the onscreen instructions to change your password.

Inspecting and Receiving Your Shipment

IMPORTANT: DO NOT OPEN THE TAMPER BAG. Your hardware will be wrapped in a tamper-evident bag with a numbered tamper seal on the closure. You will need to input the information from both the hardware unit and tamper seal into the P2PE Manager. PLEASE DO NOT REMOVE THE DEVICE FROM THE BAG UNTIL LATER.

1. At the top of the page, click on the Shipments tab. On this page, you will see all of the shipments that have been sent to you from Bluefin.

2. Click the View button next to the listed device you would like to receive.

3. Next, click the Receive button under the Received column. This will take you to the inspection page.

4a. Locate the label number on the Security Seal on the outside of the bag. Then, without opening the bag, look through the plastic to find the serial number on the bottom of the device. Enter both the serial number and tamper label number into the corresponding fields.

4b. If you are ready to activate the device immediately and process payments, check the selection for Auto Activate device, and then click Receive.

If you are not ready to activate the device at this time, then leave the Auto Activate device box unchecked, and click Receive. This puts the device into a "stored" state.

Note: You will not be able to use your device until it is in Activating status.

Activating Your Device

1. Click the Devices tab at the top of the page. Find the device you would like to deploy and click the Edit button to the left of the row item.

2. Change the Device State to Activating in the drop-down menu and press Save. Your device is now ready to be used. Now you are ready to open the bag and plug in your new P2PE device.

Note: Once you begin processing cards, your device will automatically change from Activating to Activated Status.
