RepairQ has introduced a revamped discount system designed to create custom promotions and offers. With this update, you have full control over discount amounts, durations, and the specific products and services they apply to. Discounts are applied based on predefined parameters, so they cannot apply if it does not align with a ticket item’s attribute. Additionally, users will now see discounts alongside ticket items, providing clear, visual confirmation that a discount has been applied to an item.

This enhanced flexibility allows you to attract new customers, reward loyalty, and boost sales with tailored discounts. Benefits include:

  • Enhanced Discount Control - Admins can now set specific parameters, ensuring discounts are only applied to intended items or services.

  • Strategic Promotions - Create time-sensitive offers to drive urgency and increase sales during specific periods. Tailored discounts can attract new customers and encourage repeat business, ultimately boosting sales.

  • Clear Visuals: Users can easily see which discounts have been applied to which items, enhancing ticket transparency.

  • Pro-Rata Distribution - Apply a proportional allocation when applying a dollar-amount discount across all items on a ticket.

Note: The new Discount system is optional. If you prefer the previous method of applying discounts, you can continue manually adding discounts at your discretion for any item or service, within their maximum discount limit. When the new Discount feature is active, new discounts must be created for your staff to apply to products and services. The Max Discount field in a staff member’s profile will continue to cap the amount they can discount, however, they must select a discount from the Discount List. This flexibility allows you to transition at your own pace.

Enabling Discount Use

On the top Navigation Bar go to Settings → Company Settings. On the Settings Overview page, scroll down to the Discounts section and click General Settings.

On the Discount General Settings page, you can enable and customize discount behaviors. After making your selections, be sure to Save your settings.

  • Enable - This toggle will activate/deactivate the discount feature.

  • Use Pro-rata Distribution - This feature applies a proportional allocation when applying a dollar-amount discount across all items on a ticket.

    • For instance, if you apply a $10 discount on a ticket with two items: Accessory - $20 and Labor - $50, the distribution of the discount will be proportionate to the item's price. The $20 item receives a discount of $2.86, and the $50 item receives a discount of $7.14. ($2.86 + $7.14 = $10.)

    • When inactive, dollar discounts are applied to one item at a time. If the discount amount exceeds the price of an item, the remainder will carry over to another item, etc.

  • Root Discount Category - (Optional) This allows you to apply a discount exclusively to items under the assigned Root Category. For more information on Categories and item management, check out our article: Creating an Item Tree.

  • Discount Application Priority - When adding multiple discounts to products on a ticket, you can customize the priority of how these discounts are applied. Examples are available when hovering on the tooltip next to each selection.

    • As applied to the ticket - This will calculate discounts in the order they were applied to the ticket.

    • Largest discount for customer - This will calculate the discounts in an order that will offer the largest discount.

    • Smallest discount for customer - This will calculate the discounts in an order that will offer the smallest discount.
