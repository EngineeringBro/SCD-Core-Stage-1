RepairQ Trial Database Creation

Fill Out the Registration Form and Sign Up

Head to our Pricing Page to research and select a plan, then click Start Trial to fill out the form to access your free trial.

  • Provide your name, email, and other required details.

  • Create a strong, unique password for your account security.

  • Once completed, click the Submit button to proceed.

Email Verification

After submission, you'll be redirected to a screen informing you that an email has been sent for verification purposes. Please check your inbox for our message with the subject, “RepairQ Email Validation.”

Note: Check your spam folder if you don’t see the email after a few minutes!

Verify Your Email

Open the email from us titled "RepairQ Email Validation." Click the Verify Email link contained within the email.

You'll be directed to a confirmation screen, acknowledging your email verification.

Receive Your Login Details

Shortly after verifying your email, you'll receive a second email. Please check your inbox for our message with the subject, “Welcome to RepairQ.” This one contains your database URL along with your login details.

Note: Keep this information safe as it contains your unique URL and login details.

Log In to Your Account

Click the link provided in the email to navigate to the login screen. Type in your username and password as provided in the email. Hit the Login button to access your new environment.

Note: Be sure to bookmark your login page for easy access!

Welcome to Your New Environment!

You are now logged in and ready to explore all that RepairQ offers. Dive into the features and tools available to make the most out of your trial experience.

Expired Trials and Converting to a Paid Subscription

When your trial expires, you’ll be directed to the Account Management page and be notified that your account is inactive. You’ll need to select a plan to activate your account. Click Select Plan to begin the signup process.

If your trial is still active, and you’re ready to start using RepairQ full-time, head to Settings → Company Settings to access your RepairQ account management page and subscribe to a plan before your trial ends.

On the Account Management page, you’ll see how much longer you have left on your trial period. Click Select Plan to continue activating your account.

Choose your preferred plan with Monthly pricing, then click Select Plan to continue with your billing information.

Note: The Essential Plan and above also qualify for discounted, annual pricing.

Confirm you have selected the correct plan, enter your payment details, select any optional add-ons, and then hit Subscribe to transition to our paid subscription.

Note: Be sure to apply promotional coupons you may receive before subscribing.
