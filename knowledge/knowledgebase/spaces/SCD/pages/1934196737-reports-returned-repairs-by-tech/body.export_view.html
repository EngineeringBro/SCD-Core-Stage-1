The Returned Repairs by Tech Report gives you a list of all your repairs that resulted in a Return (Rework) Repair. The report is grouped by the Staff Member responsible for the original repair, the device repaired, and the service performed. This report provides insight into the quality of repairs being performed by a technician and which devices and/or services they might be struggling with.

Viewing the Returned Repairs by Tech Report

On the Navigation Bar, click Reports. On the Reports page, click on the Productivity dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Returned Repairs by Tech.

Use the available filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters. Once you have set your filters, click Apply Filters to run the report.

  • Date Range - Select a date range for your scope of results.

  • Ticket Status - Select a specific ticket status or all ticket statuses for your results. The results will reflect the date when the ticket was updated. By default, the report will run for tickets in Closed status because this is the most reliable method for this business model.

  • Action - Filter results by any assigned Ticket Action(s).

  • Resolution - Filter results by any assigned Ticket Resolution(s).

  • Location - Select a specific location or an aggregate report of all locations.

  • Item Type - Select a specific item type or all item types.

  • Catalog Item - Keyword search for a specific catalog item.

Returned Repairs by Tech

The Returned Repairs by Tech Report looks at repairs that needed to be performed again under a return repair. The report gives a list of Repair Devices and parts used, grouped by the Technician that performed the original repair. The report also includes details for Cost, Labor cost, Replacement Charges, Waived Charges, and estimated Gain/Loss of the rework. You can hover over any of the Tooltips next to the column titles to view additional info that contributed to each calculation.

(Optional) Click Hide/Show Filters on the upper-right side of the page to collapse/expand the filters when viewing reports.

(Optional) Click To Excel on the upper-right side of the report page to export an Excel-friendly document. The report will include any filtered data when running the report.
