Whether you’re launching a new venture or transitioning from another point-of-sale system, adapting to repair-based management software can be a major process. Our onboarding plans provide the necessary tools to understand our software design, ensuring that you can take full advantage of our many features!

  • Onboarding DIY: Free - Our comprehensive Knowledge Base is loaded with articles and videos, enabling a hassle-free DIY setup. Set up RepairQ at your own pace and convenience!

  • Onboarding Custom Support: $99/hr - Need assistance with a specific task like importing your catalog or inventory? Our team can schedule dedicated time with you or a staff member to get started.

  • Onboarding Quick Start: $299 - This is a great entry point to learn how to use some of the more advanced, in-depth features of RepairQ. Your business will benefit from 2 hours of training and configuration plus 2 additional hours with a dedicated support contact.

  • Onboarding Pro: $399 - This plan is perfect for any business owner trying to get a jumpstart on getting their shop up and running. We provide 4 hours of training and configuration plus 2 additional hours with a dedicated support contact, ensuring that you can focus on growing your profits as soon as possible.
