PAX device payment processing - recovering from network connection failures

If your network connection fails between the PAX device and the RepairQ terminal while processing a payment, RepairQ may not receive the payment details from the device. In this scenario you could have a payment processed that RepairQ does not receive, and therefore is unable to save on the ticket.

With this update we added a new process that automatically recovers from intermittent connection failures. When a network connection failure occurs, RepairQ will still fetch the processed payment and save it on the ticket (provided network connection is re-established while you are still working with the ticket).
