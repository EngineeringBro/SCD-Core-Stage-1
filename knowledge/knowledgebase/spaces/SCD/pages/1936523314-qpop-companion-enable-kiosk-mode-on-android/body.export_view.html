Qpop is a customer-facing application designed to enhance interaction and engagement at the point of sale through a dedicated touchscreen interface. Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by:

  • Displaying in-store advertisements and promotions

  • Showing real-time ticket details for customer review

  • Presenting electronic forms and waivers for acknowledgment

  • Capturing electronic signatures for payments via integrated devices

An Android tablet works well as a kiosk device, allowing you to restrict it to a single app for your business. The Kiosk Browser Lockdown app can help keep the focus on the Qpop Companion app by preventing navigation to other apps or pages.

Note: This app is not required to use QP Companion, and we do not own or manage Kiosk Browser Lockdown. Please use it at your discretion.

Download the Kiosk Browser Lockdown Application

On your tablet, navigate to the Google Play store and search for Kiosk Browser Lockdown. Install the application. Open the application and follow the instructions to ‘always select Kiosk Browser as your Home Page.’

On the Kiosk Browser, tap Options and then tap Settings. Enter the default password: 0000 then tap OK.

On the Settings page, tap Admin and change the password to a memorable, secure password. Save and go back to Settings.

On the settings page, go to General → Kiosk URL. Enter the URL for your database. Save your changes and exit the settings page.

When you exit Settings, you will be routed to your new launch screen, where you can log in and pair your Qpop Companion.

Note: If you choose to keep the tablet in always-on status, make sure your device is plugged into a power source.
