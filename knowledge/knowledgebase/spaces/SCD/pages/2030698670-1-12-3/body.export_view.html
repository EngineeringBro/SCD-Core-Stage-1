Qpop Requests When Disconnected

When your Qpop companion is offline and you try to send a payment signature request or a custom form request, RepairQ will respond with a more helpful status window.

Qpop Pairing & Connectivity Improvements

Both Qpop and RepairQ terminals will handle disconnections more gracefully, automatically re-establishing a connection when possible and retaining pairing with more reliability.

iOS Qpop app

When running Qpop as an app (instead of in the browser), clicking the cancel button on payment signatures or custom forms would open the page in Safari instead of inside the Qpop app screen. This has been fixed.

Qpop Custom Forms

We increased the portion of the screen that displays custom form & waiver content.
