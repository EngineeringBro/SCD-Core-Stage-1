Released 06/29/2017 1:57PM CT (18:57 UTC)

Refurbish Ticket Label Barcodes

Refurb ticket labels were not showing/hiding a barcode based on ticket label settings. This issue has been resolved.

Qpop™Ad Rotator

Under some conditions the Qpop idle screen ad rotator would not begin rotating after returning from the ticket summary. This issue has been resolved.

Announcement Emails

The Email and Dashboard display rules were not always enforced properly. Now email notifications will be sent when email is included, and announcements will show on the dashboard when the dashboard view is included.

Report Catalog Item Searches

Special characters in the catalog item search no longer prevent the report from filtering properly.

Location Overrides Export

The location overrides export has been optimized. When exporting dozens or hundreds of locations worth of location overrides, the export will now succeed without  reaching memory limits.

Custom Form HTML Field

Submitting multiple edits at different times on a custom form that has an HTML field with formatting would sometimes duplicate the HTML formatting (e.g. bullet lists) with each edit. This issue has been resolved, and formatting will remain consistent through multiple edits.
