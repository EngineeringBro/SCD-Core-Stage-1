The first step of using RepairQ is signing into the system. If you are the business owner and this is your first time logging in, you will need to reset your password, so you can establish a new PIN/Password to log in. Users can also add a photo to their Staff Profile using gravatar.

Note: For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome browser. You can also use Safari, Firefox, and Edge browsers.

Log In to RepairQ

Open your web browser and navigate to your store's URL. Your RepairQ URL will be similar to https://www.yourbusiness.repairq.io.

On the login screen, enter your username and PIN/Password, then click Login.

Note: If there are multiple locations for this database, make sure to select the correct location before logging in.

After logging in, you will be directed to the Home Page, where you can view alerts, view information about the Knowledge Base, and begin your daily tasks.

Log Out of RepairQ

After you are finished using the application, we recommend logging out to prevent other users from performing tasks under your credentials. On the right side of the Navigation Bar, click the Name dropdown. From the list of options, click Logout. Your session will end, and you will be taken to the login screen.

Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your PIN/Password or are the business owner and this is your first time logging in, you can reset your password via an email link.

Open your web browser and navigate to your store's URL. Your RepairQ URL will be similar to https://businessname.repairq.io or 401b-ca1001.repairq.io. On the login screen, click the "Forgot Password?" link next to the Login button.

Enter the email address associated with your Staff Profile and click Recover. You should receive an email with a link to reset your password within a few minutes. If you don't see the email, be sure to check your spam folder. Follow the link and establish a new PIN/Password. Your PIN can be numeric or alphanumeric, based on your preference. After you have established a new PIN, you can log into RepairQ.

Add a Photo to Your Profile

If you would like to add a photo to your Staff Profile, you can add an image using Gravatar.

After logging into RepairQ, click your Name Dropdown on the right side of the Navigation Bar. From the list of options, click Profile.

On the Your Profile page, click the link under the image: “Add your own picture at Gravatar.com.” You will be taken to Gravatar, where you can create an account for free and link your image.

Note: The profile page also includes quick links to create a Sale, Repair, or Trade-in Ticket and a link to the Ticket Queue.
