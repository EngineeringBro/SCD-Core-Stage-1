Users can save Quick Sale, Repair, and Trade-in tickets as quotes, which enables tickets to be saved and re-opened later without affecting inventory levels. Quotes also use the same status system that you use for tickets and repairs. Converting a quote into an active ticket is a one-click process, and once converted, the related inventory is immediately pulled to the ticket.

  • When negotiating a repair, sale, or trade-in with a customer, a staff member can save a quote and leave the ticket while the customer shops or considers a deal. Previously, a trade-in ticket had to be closed with a transaction before the user could leave the ticket.

    • Inventory items added to the ticket are not pulled from inventory, and trade-in items added to the ticket will not be added to inventory.

  • Quotes are not included in revenue reports, since the transaction has not been completed. Revenue reporting only looks at Tickets in Closed status.

  • The ability to save a ticket as a quote can be toggled on/off.

    • Admins can toggle this selection at the Company level, affecting all locations, or override this decision at the Location level.

    • By default, RepairQ has this feature disabled. This article includes instructions on how to allow this feature.

Tips for saving a ticket as a quote

  • You can only save a ticket as a quote before saving it as another status. If a ticket has already been progressed to a status, it cannot be reverted to a quote.

  • Save as Quote is available in the ticket workflow as long as a customer and item have been added.

Allow “Save as Quote” for Tickets

Log into RepairQ as a user with Admin access. On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings Overview screen, scroll until you find the Tickets section. Under this category, click General Settings.

Note: An admin can enable this feature for all locations by navigating to Settings → Company Settings.

When viewing the Ticket General Settings page, turn off the toggle for ‘Disable “Save as quote” button on new tickets.’ Once you have made your selection, click Save to submit your changes.

  • Toggle this setting ON (default) to disable the ability to save tickets as quotes.

  • Toggle this setting OFF to enable saving tickets as quotes.

Convert a Ticket to a Quote

When creating a new ticket, and you link a customer and add a Repair Device or Ticket Item, the option to save the ticket as a quote will be available along the bottom row of ticket statuses.

The quote will be saved in New status and you can find the ticket in Quote List or by filtering the Ticket List for quotes.
