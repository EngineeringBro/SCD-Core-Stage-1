There may be a scenario where a customer comes in for an exchange, and the new item they choose costs less than the original purchase item. This process will demonstrate how to perform the exchange on the original ticket and issue a refund for the difference back to the customer.

Exchange an Item and Refund the Difference (Partial Refund)

A VCT must be assigned to the terminal to perform a refund on a ticket.

 

Return the Item

Search for the closed ticket in your Ticket Queue. On the Ticket View page, click the Return Button along the row of actions on the upper-right section of the page.

When the Start Return pop-up appears, click Start a Return. This will re-open the ticket for editing.

In the Return Items section of the ticket, select the appropriate item(s) you wish to return. If the returned item is inventoried, place it back into the appropriate stock status. Add notes for the item and click Continue.

In the Ticket Items section of the ticket, add the new item to the ticket. The Ticket Totals section will now show a pending refund amount for the customer.

Refund the Purchase

After updating the ticket, the Totals section will show the refund amount due to the customer. On the Transaction section of the ticket, click the Refund button next to the original transaction.

When the Add Transaction pop-up appears, select the original Payment Type and enter the refund amount as a positive value. Ensure the Refund In Full checkbox is unchecked. After entering the required information and any additional notes, click Submit Refund to proceed.

Verify the transaction as posted to the ticket and that the payment has been issued back to the customer. Once the balance due is $0.00, you can Save the ticket to finalize the process.
