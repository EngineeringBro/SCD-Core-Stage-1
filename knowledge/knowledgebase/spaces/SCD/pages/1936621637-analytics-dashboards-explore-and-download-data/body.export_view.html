RepairQ includes a powerful Business Intelligence (BI) Suite, provided through integration with Looker. This tool offers a set of visual dashboards covering key performance indicators (KPIs) essential for running a retail repair business. These reports allow you to quickly view important aspects of your business, such as sales trends, inventory levels, and repair progress, helping you make more informed decisions.

The Business Intelligence Suite is getting even better with a new feature allowing you to explore the underlying data in your reports. Each report allows viewing and analyzing data from various perspectives using filters and alternative visualizations. Additionally, you can download report charts, graphs, or the underlying data in several formats for use outside of our application.

Our Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboards are available to Admin users on our Advanced and Professional plans. Users on the Advanced Plan can view a wide range of pre-built reports and dashboards, giving them access to critical insights about their business. However, they are limited to viewing these pre-made reports and cannot create new ones. Professional Plan users can view pre-built reports and build custom reports. This allows them to tailor reports to their specific needs and gain deeper insights from their data.

Explore Your Data

On the RepairQ Navigation Bar, go to Reports → Analytics. When viewing the Daily Snapshot, use the Dashboards dropdown to select the appropriate dashboard. You can dive deeper into each report by clicking the small Gear Icon on any dashboard widget and selecting ‘Explore From Here.’ This action opens up additional fields, filters, and visualizations, allowing you to further analyze your data.

Change Visualizations

For this report, a Line graph offers a clear visual representation. In the Visualization section, click Line, and the report will update. You'll see two lines: one representing your daily sales average and the other showing the total sales for each day. Choose the visualization that best represents the report data. Different visualizations highlight various aspects of the data, so select the one that provides the clearest insights for your needs.

Download Your Data

From each dashboard or widget, you can click the Gear Icon in the top right and select Download Data to download either the report visualization or the underlying data.
