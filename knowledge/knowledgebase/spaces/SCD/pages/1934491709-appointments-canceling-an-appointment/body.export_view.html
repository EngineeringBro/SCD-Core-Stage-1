Users can reserve blocks of time for customers to come in and spend time with an assigned user. You can also specify whether this appointment is meant for a Repair ticket, Sales ticket, or a Trade-in ticket. If a customer decides they no longer need their reservation, users can cancel the appointment from the Appointment List.

Locating the Appointment List

To find your Appointment List, use the Navigation Bar at the top of RepairQ to go to Opportunities → Appointments.

Viewing Set Appointments

After an appointment is set, it will appear on the Appointments List View page. 

The Appointments List View page allows users to search for appointments by Name, Device, Request Type, and more. For more on how list views and searching work, please see our article, Custom List Views, Filters, and Columns.

You can view scheduled appointments by List, Day, Week, or Month. In each view, you will notice a left and right arrow that allow you to cycle between the unit of time. There is also a today button, which when viewing the Day style will bring you back to the current date.

Canceling an Appointment

We'll walk you through a quick example using Bob Roberts' name as a keyword to narrow results to find their November 28th appointment and cancel it. 

1. From the Appointments List View filter, select +Keyword.

2. Enter your keyword (Bob Roberts), then select Apply.

3. From the narrowed results, scroll through to find the appointment you would like to cancel and click the Options Menu below the Action column.

4. From the Options Menu, select Cancel. When you are finished, the list view will refresh, and the appointment will be canceled and removed from the appointment list.

Note: You can view canceled appointments by selecting the filter Status - Canceled.
