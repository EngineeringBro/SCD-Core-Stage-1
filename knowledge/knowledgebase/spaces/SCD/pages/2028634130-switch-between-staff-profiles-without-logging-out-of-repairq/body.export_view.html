Switch User Profiles in RepairQ (Fast Switching)

When the feature is active, if someone else is currently logged into RepairQ, you can swap users without logging out of the application. Admins can update authentication username/pin requirements for fast switching in Staff Management Settings.

 

On the right side of the Navigation Bar, click the Name dropdown. From the list of options, click Switch User.

A Switch User pop-up will appear. Depending on your preferred settings, enter your Username and/or PIN/Passcode and then click the Switch button.

You will now be logged under the new Staff Profile. You can verify by checking the name on the top right of the Navigation Bar.
