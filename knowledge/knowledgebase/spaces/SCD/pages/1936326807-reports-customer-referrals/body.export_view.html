Summary/Details by Customer Referral

Users can run Customer Referral reports that will show sales data for each Referral Method. RepairQ also offers detailed reporting by referral. You can filter your results by date, Ticket Status, Location, Item Types, or specific Catalog Items.

Note: We recommend users filter these reports based on Closed tickets only. Once a ticket is closed, the date will no longer change, and the ticket has been paid in full. This also aligns with our Accounting Principles, as revenue is recorded once the ticket is fully closed.

Viewing Reports by Customer Referral

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Reports.

2. On the Reports page, click on the Sales dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Summary by Referral.

Note: You can view a detailed list of results including the Customer's Name, Ticket Date, and Sales details by selecting Details by Referral.

3. Use the available filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters.

  • Date Range - Set a date range for your scope of results.

  • Ticket Status - Search by Ticket Status. We recommend Closed as the default status for accrual accounting purposes.

  • Location - Select the location(s) for customer referral data results.

  • Item Type - Search for a specific type or all Item Types in your Catalog. Leave this field unselected to search all types.

  • Catalog Item - Search for specific Catalog Items by keyword.

4. Once you have set your filters, click Apply Filters to run the report.

Summary by Referral

Sales Summary by Referral will show combined sales data for each Customer Referral Method. If you would like to see a breakdown of customer and sales data, click Detail Report.

Details by Referral

Sales Details by Referral will show sales data for each Referral Method. If you would like to see a summary of these results, click Summary Report.
