Summary/Details by Customer Segment

Users can run Customer Segment reports that will show sales data for each Customer Segment.

Note: We recommend users filter these reports based on Closed tickets only. Once a ticket is closed, the date will no longer change, and the ticket has been paid in full. This also aligns with our Accounting Principles, as revenue is recorded once the ticket is fully closed.

Viewing Reports by Customer Segment

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Reports.

2. On the Reports page, click on the Sales dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Summary by Customer Segment.

Note: You can view a detailed list of results including the Customer's Name, Ticket Date, and Sales details by selecting Details by Customer Segment.

3. Use the available filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters.

  • Date Range - Set a date range for your scope of results.

  • Ticket Status - Search by Ticket Status. We recommend Closed as the default status for accrual accounting purposes.

  • Location - Select the location(s) for customer segment data results.

  • Ticket Item Type - Search by none, any, or all Item Types in your Catalog.

  • Catalog Item - Search for specific Catalog Items by keyword.

4. Once you have set your filters, click Apply Filters.

  

Summary by Customer Segment

Summary by Customer Segment will show combined sales data for each Customer Segment. If you would like to see a breakdown customer and sales data, click Detail Report.

Details by Customer Segment

Details by Customer Segment will show sales data for each Customer Segment, further sorted by each Customer under the respective segment. If you would like to see a summary of these results, click Summary Report.
