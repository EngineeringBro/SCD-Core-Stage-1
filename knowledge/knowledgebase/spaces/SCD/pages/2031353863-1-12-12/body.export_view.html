Backed by popular demand, when copy & pasting information into the problem/diagnostics area formatting is now stripped and only content (text) is pasted in. 

Integrated Printing Optimization

We have optimized Integrated Printing for windows. If you run RepairQ with integrated printing on a lower memory windows machine, you may have experienced slow print times, or crashes during the print process. This update should dramatically alleviate these problems for low memory windows machines.
