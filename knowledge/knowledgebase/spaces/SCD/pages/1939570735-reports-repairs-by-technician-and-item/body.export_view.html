Viewing the Repairs by Technician & Item Report

On the Navigation Bar, click Reports. On the Reports page, click on the Productivity dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Repairs by Tech & Item.

Use the available filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters. Once you have set your filters, click Apply Filters to run the report.

Note: We recommend viewing productivity based on closed tickets. Once a ticket has been closed, the date will no longer change, and the ticket has been paid in full. This also aligns with our accounting principles, as open tickets are considered a liability until the ticket is closed.

  • Date Range - Select a date range for your scope of results.

  • Location - Select a specific location or an aggregate report of all locations.

  • Ticket Status - Select a specific ticket status or all ticket statuses for your results. The results will reflect the date when the ticket was updated. By default, the report will run for tickets in Closed status because this is the most reliable method for this business model.

  • Action - Filter results by any assigned Ticket Action(s).

  • Resolution - Filter results by any assigned Ticket Resolution(s).

  • Item Type - Select a specific item type or all item types.

  • Catalog Item - Keyword search for a specific catalog item.

  • Include Labor in Total Cost - Labor is added based on hours entered on ticket items and the assigned staff member's pay rate assigned in their profile.

Repairs by Technician & Item

The Repairs by Tech and Item Report provides a combined, detailed look at a technician's work, showing the number of repairs done for each Catalog Item with Cost and Profit data.

The Profit Margin is calculated by the following equation: Gross Sale - Discounts - Total Cost = Profit Margin.

(Optional) Click Hide/Show Filters on the upper-right side of the page to collapse/expand the filters when viewing reports.

Click To Excel on the upper-right side of the report page to export an Excel-friendly document. The export will include any filtered data when running the report.
