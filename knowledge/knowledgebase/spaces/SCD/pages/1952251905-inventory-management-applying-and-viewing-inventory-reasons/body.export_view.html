Admins can now require users to record why an item has been placed in defective status. This affects items received on Purchase Orders, marking an item as Defective/Damaged on tickets, and when manually editing the inventory item. Admins can configure a list of defect reasons when moving inventory items into this status. They can also assign a default reason that automatically applies when placing an item into Pending RMA status when receiving defective items on a Purchase Order.

Managers can use the Inventory Detail List to filter for items in damaged/defective status and add a new column, Inventory Update Reason, to track why inventory was counted as damaged or defective.

Applying a Defective Inventory Reason from a Ticket

When using the RMA process on a ticket, the user can mark an item as defective by clicking the Damaged/Defective icon next to the ticket item. This will open a new window to update the item as Damaged or Defective.

The user will select the item’s inventory status, and then choose the Reason for Defect. Admins create the available list of Reason for Defect selections and will identify why the item has been marked in the corresponding inventory status.

  • Defective Item - This will initiate the RMA process for that item.

  • Damaged Item - This will place the item into Damaged status to be recorded as inventory shrink.

Applying a Defective Inventory Reason from Inventory Details

You can edit an individual inventory item’s details on the Inventory Details page. When an item is changed to Pending RMA or RMA Sent status, the new field will appear to record the reason that the item was placed in that status.

Applying a Defective Inventory Reason from a Purchase Order

On the Inventory Update Reasons Overview page, the reason marked as Default on PO will automatically be applied when moving items into Defective (Pending RMA) Status on a Purchase Order.

Viewing Inventory Update Reasons on the Inventory Detail List

When viewing the Inventory Detail page, you can add a new column to view Inventory Update Reasons. Use the filter options to locate items in Defective/Damaged status and view those reasons in the corresponding columns. For more information on adding new columns, saving, and sharing list views, check out our article, Saving and Sharing Custom List Views.
