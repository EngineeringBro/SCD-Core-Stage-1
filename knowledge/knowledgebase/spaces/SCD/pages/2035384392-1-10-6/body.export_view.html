Release 02/16/2017 12:12PM CT (18:13 UTC)

Inventory Count Sheet

The scan to count input is not registering the "enter" key with every scan (the enter key submits the scan entry). This is an intermittent issue that affects some scans and not others, and it affects some barcode scanner models but not others.

We added a few measures to prevent this behavior for the troublesome barcode scanner models. With this update scanning should work smoothly and consistently going forward for anyone who has experienced trouble with this.
