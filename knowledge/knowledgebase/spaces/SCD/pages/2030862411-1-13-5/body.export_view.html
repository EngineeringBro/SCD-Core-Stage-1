Released 9/7/2017 12:19 CT (17:19 UTC)

Print Templates - new label customization options

You now have the ability to customize the label for the "Warranty Information" and "Notes" sections on the ticket receipt and invoice templates!

VCT Re-Assign Cash on Hand

The "cash on hand" field (starting cash) is not relevant when a VCT is re-assigned that is in a de-assigned (not closed) state, so we have removed the field.

Manager Custom Form Permissions

There were a few misconfigured permissions for the manager role related to custom form field editing/deleting. These are now configured as intended.
