Automated Notifications allow staff to inform coworkers or customers of changes in RepairQ that impact them. This automation reduces the time required to send communications, enabling efficient interactions between teams and enhancing the overall customer experience. Admins and Managers can tailor automated notifications to meet the specific needs of the business.

Automated Notifications streamline the communication process commonly associated with:

  • Ticket Status Updates - Automatically notify customers when their device is ready for pickup or when there are changes to their ticket status.

  • Surveys and Feedback - Send follow-up links to experience surveys after a service is completed, gathering valuable customer feedback.

  • Order Management - Inform Managers when new Purchase Orders are created, ensuring timely processing and oversight.

  • Inventory Alerts - Alert Managers when inventory items are categorized as Damaged, Shrinkage, Pending RMA, or other relevant statuses.
