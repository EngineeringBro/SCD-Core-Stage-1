Released 05/23/2017 2:56 PM CT (19:56 UTC)

Internal PO (Inventory Transfer)

The carrier and condition columns have been removed from the receiving side of inventory transfers. The status and condition will transfer unchanged with the inventory items.

Purchase Order Item List

Column widths for price/cost/status/condition will now carry a minimum width, giving you the ability to manage them more effectively on smaller screens. In addition, the purchase order item list scrolls horizontally on smaller screens so that there is sufficient room to display all of the columns.

Ticket Tax Override

The tax override checkbox would not always update the ticket immediately when checked or unchecked. This has been resolved.

Diagnostics / Notes Toolbar & Formatting

For tickets with multiple repair or tradein devices, or when adding multiple ticket notes, the text box would not always display the text formatting tools. This has been resolved.

PAX Payment Processing

Added some additional measures to prevent the intermittent "Transaction # is missing for Credit Card transaction" error when saving a payment.
