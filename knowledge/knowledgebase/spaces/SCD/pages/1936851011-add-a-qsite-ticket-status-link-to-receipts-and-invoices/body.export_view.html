Qsite provides an easy way for your customers to check their ticket status. The ticket status page shows a summary of the ticket, the current status of the ticket, the balance due, and when it was last updated.

Adding Qsite Links to Receipts and Invoices

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings.

2. On the Settings Overview page, click the Templates dropdown on the left column. From the list of options, click either Receipts or Invoices, depending on which template you would like to change.

3. On the Template Settings page, look for the Ticket Status URL section. Once you are satisfied with your changes, click Save.

  • Click the checkbox next to Show URL to print the Qsite portal URL on your receipt/invoice.

  • Click the checkbox next to Show QRcode to print a QR code with the Qsite portal URL on your receipt/invoice.

Show URL

This option adds a Qsite™ ticket status portal link to the ticket receipt or invoice.

Show QR Code

 This option adds a Qsite™ ticket status portal QR code link to the ticket receipt or invoice that the customer can scan and follow from their smartphone.
