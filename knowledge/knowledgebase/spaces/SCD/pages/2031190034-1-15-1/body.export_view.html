Limited Release on 5/22/18
Full Release 5 June 2018

Purchase Order Items - Allow Duplicates

You can now add duplicate items on a non-integrated purchase order. This was introduced to support ordering different variations or grades of the same catalog item.

Purchase Order Totals

The purchase order add/edit screen now calculates the order totals for you in the summary section.

Accounting Journal - Purchase Order Costs

The RepairQ Accounting Journal now produces entries for purchase order taxes, shipping costs, and cost adjustments.

Trade-in Item Inventory Notes

We added an inventory item note on the trade-in item intake form, and the inventory item note will be saved with the inventory item record. We also added the trade-in diagnostic note field to the intake form to speed up trade-in intake.

Overhead Rate on Profitability Report

We are changing the calculation to be simpler to manage. Instead of applying your location overhead rate * business hours, it will simply become overhead rate * hours. This should make calculating an hourly overhead rate much simpler.

Invoice Print - Customize Title

Now you can customize the Invoice and Quote print titles if you wish to relabel them.

Manufacturer Pick List Sort

On the inventory item stock and on the trade-in intake form the manufacturer list is now sorted alphabetically.

Customer Credit Transaction List

The transaction list was hidden in some cases when it should have been visible. This is now fixed.
