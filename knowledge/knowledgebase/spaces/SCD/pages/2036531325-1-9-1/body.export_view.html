This is a minor update to address bugs and add minor features.

Credit Card Quick Swipe

The legacy "quick swipe" field for processing credit cards using the IDTECH SREDKey or M130 device could get "stuck" on the loading indicator if you had multiple Credit Card payment methods enabled on a VCT. This is fixed, and having multiple credit card payment methods enabled on a VCT should no longer cause any problems.
