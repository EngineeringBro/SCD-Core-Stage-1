As the year ends, many stores must provide a complete inventory valuation for accounting and tax purposes. The Inventory Valuation Report enables users to capture the total value (i.e., cost) of on-hand inventory at a specific moment.

The Inventory End-of-Year (EOY) Snapshot archive is a valuable tool for stores needing a complete inventory valuation for accounting or tax purposes. It provides a downloadable document listing all items with an active inventory status as of December 31st, including quantity, inventory status, cost, and more. Snapshots are available on January 1st of the new year.

We recommend taking periodic "snapshots" of your inventory and its valuation to maintain accurate historical records. These snapshots capture a precise point-in-time inventory value which can be used for later reference. Remember to run the report on the exact date and time you want to capture.

Note: Since this feature was implemented in 2022, users cannot pull reports before this year.

View the End of Year (EOY) Snapshot Archive

On the Navigation Bar, click Reports. On the Reports page, click on the Inventory dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Inventory EOY Snapshot.

Use the filters to select your location and year then click Download Report. The report downloads as an Excel-compatible document, listing all items with an active inventory status as of December 31st of the previous year. It includes details such as quantity, inventory status, cost, and more.
