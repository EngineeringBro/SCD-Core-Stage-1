Released 06/06/2018 1:40pm CT (18:40 UTC)

List Views

List views are now more condensed on smaller screen sizes by leveraging more text-wrapping within columns and dynamically resizing the actions column.

Bold text on ticket notes

We have added a new font variation to support bolded text better.

Checkbox clickable area update

The checkbox clickable area was too wide (full width of the container). Now the clickable area of a checkbox is just the checkbox, and sometimes the label as well.

Catalog Item Edit - Save Button 

To improve usability, the save button will now "stick" to the top of the screen when you scroll past it so that it is always visible.
