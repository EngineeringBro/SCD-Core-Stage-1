1.17.15 is a minor update including some small features and bug fixes.

Scheduled 11:30 CT on December 7, 2020

New Customer Search Algorithm

  • We added a new customer search algorithm that is much faster at returning matching results. The new algorithm should also rank more relevant matches higher in the list of results - one exception being if you have a custom sort applied on the customer list view.

  • You can try out the new algorithm by turning off the "legacy customer search" in your Miscellaneous Company Settings. When enabled, the new algorithm will affect the customer list view (and global customer search) as well as the Ticket customer search.

Inventory Performance Improvements

  • We added a number of minor performance improvements to the inventory list views. You do not need to do anything to enable this.

Bug fixes

  • We fixed issue where the ticket tax rate override was not overriding all applicable tax rates.

  • We restored the original catalog item template download to make it easier to create imports for new items
