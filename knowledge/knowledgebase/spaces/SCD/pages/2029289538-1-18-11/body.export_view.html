Released 09/26/2022 at 11:30pm CT

Minor Changes

  • The Rev/Rallio integration now looks for either a phone number or an email address where, prior, both were required.

  • Improved the Payment Request Modal UI for readability and usability.

  • Square is now disabled when the Square account is disconnected from RepairQ.

  • Added a setting to toggle Tip Amount (found under Settings → Templates → Receipts) option which allows customers to see the amount they tipped on their receipt and / or invoice.

  • Added the ability to see Change Log history for catalog item compatibility .

Bug Fixes

  • Resolved an issue that stops Square devices from functioning after a device code reset.

  • Refurbishment tickets now retain their items' original costs.

  • Instock issue, which could sometimes visually be absent in the Inventory Summary, now displays correctly again.
