RepairQ empowers business owners by providing robust accounting and inventory reports tailored to extract essential data for your accounting system. These reports streamline the process of posting data to your accounting software and enhance your ability to make informed decisions based on accurate and detailed financial information.

  • Efficiency - Quickly extract crucial data, saving time and effort in manually gathering and inputting information into your accounting system.

  • Accuracy - Ensure precision in your accounting records by providing detailed reports, reducing the risk of errors associated with manual data entry.

  • Financial Insight - RepairQ offers a range of reports, including accounting summaries, journal entries, revenue and COGS, inventory usage, inventory valuation, and more.

  • Compliance - Meet accounting standards and compliance requirements by providing organized and detailed data for your accounting system and accountant.

  • Integration - Our integration with QuickBooks Online allows you to automate the transmission of nightly journal entries, freeing up your time to focus on core business activities.

Exporting Reports in RepairQ

Reports can be exported using the To Excel button near the top-right of the page.

Accounting Reports in RepairQ

1. On the top Navigation Bar, click Reports.

2. On the Reports page, click the Accounting dropdown to view numerous reports designed to extract information you can post to an accounting system.

Accounting Summary Report

The Summary Report is the best place to look for most figures you would post to your accounting system. This report was designed with the help of several accounts in the industry to gather relevant data from RepairQ. For recommendations on where to post these figures to a chart of accounts, see our article, RepairQ Accounting Principles.

Payment and Revenue Reporting

The Tickets Unpaid and Pending Revenue Reports track tickets that still require payment (Tickets Unpaid) and tickets that haven't been closed for final reporting (Pending Revenue).

COGS Reporting

The Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) Report, along with other revenue-based reports can be used to extract detailed data regarding Revenue and COGS reporting

Inventory Reports in RepairQ

On the Reports page, click the Inventory column to display numerous reports organized to extract information you can post to an accounting system.

Inventory Valuation Reports

Use the Inventory Valuation Reports to view a snapshot of your current inventory value, inventory movements, and details.

Inventory Usage Reports

The Inventory Usage Reports are designed to give you insight into inventory that has been consumed (no longer in your possession), whether through a sale, RMA process, shrinkage, etc.

Inventory End of Year (EOY) Snapshot Archive

The Inventory EOY Snapshot report provides a downloadable document that includes a list of all items in an active inventory status on the last day of the previous year. This report is available for the end of 2022 and will grow as time passes.
