In RepairQ, a Billing Agent is the entity that assumes financial responsibility for products and services provided to a third party. Billing Agents are usually businesses, organizations, schools, and warranty providers. Invoicing gives your company tools to manage customers and corporate clients with net terms and is available to users on our Advanced and Professional plans.

Assigning a Billing Agent to a Ticket

After attaching a customer to a ticket, you can manually assign a billing agent under the Customer & Billing Agent section.

Assign a Billing Agent to a Customer

If you have a customer that will consistently use a specific Billing Agent, you can link that information under their Customer Profile. Going forward, each time this customer is added to a ticket, the Billing Agent details will automatically be assigned.

1. Customer Search that you wish to assign a Billing Agent and view their Customer Profile.

2. On the right side of the page, click the Edit button to make changes to their profile.

3. On the Edit Customer pop-up, click the Billing tab on the left column. Under the Billing Agent section, select the agent you would like to assign to this customer. After making your selection, click the Save button to finalize your changes.

4. When the customer is added to a ticket, Billing Agent details will automatically be assigned.

Note: If the customer has a ticket that will not be billed to the Billing Agent, you can remove the agent details from the ticket.
