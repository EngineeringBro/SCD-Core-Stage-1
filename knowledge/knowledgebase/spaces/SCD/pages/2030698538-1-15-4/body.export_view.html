New Item Bundling Settings

With this update, we are expanding bundling beyond the strict pairing of labor/services and parts. Now you can bundle any item under a labor/service item or under a part using the following two new settings:

  • Allow Bundling under Part (as child)

  • Allow Bundling under Labor (as child)

What can you do with this?

Now you can bundle accessories, additional labor items, or anything else you need to track with your labor/service charges. You could also bundle multiple parts or consumables with a single part line item charge. You could of course do other things with it as well, these are just a few examples!
