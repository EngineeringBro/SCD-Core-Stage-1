RepairQ utilizes QZ Tray integration for seamless printing without navigating print dialogs. This provides users with a one-click printing process for receipts, invoices, and labels directly to their assigned printer. Users can also create and customize print automation for a hands-free process for printing, further streamlining your ticket workflow.

  • Effortless Printing - Skip the hassle of print dialogs and save time by generating receipts, invoices, and labels, through a single-click process.

  • Streamlined Workflow - Automated Printing saves time and effort by reducing manual printing tasks and creating a smoother experience for the user and customer.

  • Personalized Print Automation - Customize automation by directing different document types to specific printers (receipt, desk, label).

Before installing QZ Tray, make sure all of your computers using this integration meet the Minimum System Requirements. Once you have verified, continue below for setup resources. Once you have completed this process, please see our additional resources for further integration setup and troubleshooting.

Downloading and Installing QZ

By default, RepairQ sends print jobs to your printer through a browser dialog. To print from your browser, you need to connect your printer to your computer. This step exists outside of RepairQ since the pairing exists between your OS and your printer. If you need assistance with this process, check out our Recommended Hardware Resources.

1. Log in to the location you would like to activate integrated printing. From the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings.

2. On the Settings Overview page, click Integrations under the Account section. Alternatively, you can find Integrations under the Account settings dropdown on the left column.

3. On the Integration page, look for the Available Integrations settings and click QZ Printing.

4. On the QZ Integration page, click Download QZ. This will open a new window with download options for your operating system. Windows, Mac OS, and Linux are all supported.

5. Download the latest version of the QZ Tray application — v2.2.1. We recommend downloading approved versions, rather than pre-release versions, so if you need you download a previous version of QZ, see the QZ Archive page.

6. Open the downloaded installer to install QZ Tray on your computer. The installer will walk you through the installation steps.

7. Once you have installed the QZ Tray application on your computer, the application should start running. Depending on your computer's OS, you will see a small QZ receipt printer icon on your application tray/status bar.

8. Click the QZ Tray Icon and select About to get your current version details.

Windows 10 and Higher

macOS

9. Go back to your QZ Integration settings in RepairQ. Check the box next to Enable Integrated Printing. Next, select the QZ Tray Version that matches your installed version, and click Save QZ Settings.

10. When navigating to Settings → Location Settings, the Settings Overview page includes new options for Integrated Printing. The dropdown has new sections for adding your Integrated Printers, creating Print Configurations, and customizing Print Automation.
