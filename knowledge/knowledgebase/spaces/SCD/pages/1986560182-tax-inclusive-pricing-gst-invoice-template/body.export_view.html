Print Templates allow you to choose which information appears on invoices and receipts. Certain information may be required on these documents for companies subject to GST/VAT or similar tax laws. This article will guide you through updating these details in RepairQ to ensure they are visible to your customers.

In the examples below, we'll focus on GST-friendly labeling. However, you can customize the names of these fields according to your preferences. Please note that these changes are purely visual and will not affect the pricing calculations.

Include Tax ID and Tax-Inclusive Info on Invoices

On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings Overview page, under the Templates category, click Invoices.

On the Invoice Template page, you can customize which details will be printed on your invoices. Use your discretion to include relevant fields that best suit your needs. For this demonstration, we will focus on highlighting GST-specific fields. Once you’ve made your customizations, don't forget to click Save to apply your changes.

Invoice Settings

  • Invoice Title - Assign a title for your invoice that will be visible when printing.

    • For GST customers, you can name this as 'Tax Invoice.'

  • Ticket Items - Select this option to include a line detail for ticket items.

  • Line Item Price/Tax Display - Select how line item prices and taxes appear on the invoice.

    • For GST Customers, we recommend selecting Tax-inclusive: show tax-inclusive price, effective after-tax discount, etc.

  • Tax-Inclusive Unit Price (Before Discount) - This includes the price of all items on the ticket before discounts are applied.

    • For GST, we recommend using ‘Subtotal (Inc GST).’

  • Effective AFter-tax Discount - The total of any discounts applied toward ticket items.

    • For GST, we recommend using ‘Discount (Inc GST).’

  • Tax - This is the total for all taxes applied to the ticket items.

    • For GST, we recommend using ‘GST.’

Ticket Totals

  • Tax-Inclusive Ticket Total Without Discounts - This includes the price of all items on the ticket before discounts are applied.

    • For GST, we recommend using ‘Subtotal (Inc GST).’

  • Total Effective After-Tax Discount - The total of any discounts applied toward ticket items.

    • For GST, we recommend using 'Discount (Inc GST).'

Viewing an Invoice After Customization

After saving your template, you can print an invoice from the Ticket View page. The fields will be updated to reflect your recent changes.

This example demonstrates how the column names and labels appear after updating the invoice template:

  • Invoice Settings

    • Invoice Title - Invoice → Tax Invoice

  • Ticket Items

    • Tax-Inclusive Unit Price (Before Discount) - Price → (Inc GST)

    • Effective AFter-tax Discount - Discount → Discount (Inc GST)

    • Tax - Tax → GST

  • Ticket Totals

    • Tax-Inclusive Ticket Total Without Discounts - Subtotal → Subtotal (Inc GST)

    • Total Effective After-Tax Discount - Discount → Discount (Inc GST)
