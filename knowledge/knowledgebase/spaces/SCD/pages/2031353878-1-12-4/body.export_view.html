Released 04/12/17 9:49PM CT

Internal Purchase Orders (transfers)

  • Internal transfers are now fully disabled if only one location is active

  • Inactive locations no longer show in the internal supplier list

  • Inventory managers can now print packing slips

  • Added a "view serials" interface to view serial numbers for inventory transferred

Catalog Item Labels

Display location override price on catalog item labels.

Reconcile all credit cards on VCT

Fixed and enhanced the feature that allows non-integrated credit cards to be reconciled on a single VCT.

Inactive Item Types

Exclude catalog items from searches and list views where the parent Item Type is inactive.

Max Discount Allowed for Manager Role

The manager role now no longer grants the ability to grant 100% discount on tickets, unless no max discount is defined on their staff profile. An admin can set the max discount that a manager may grant with the  "max discount" field, and it will be enforced as it is enforced with other user roles.

New Loading Indicator

We added a new loading indicator for your viewing pleasure.

Invoice Filters

The "customer" and "billing agent" filters on the invoice screen were not filtering as intended. These filters have been updated, and they will filter results with a stricter logic.

Branded Logos on Email Receipts

Some of you have had trouble getting logos to show up for emailed receipts and invoices. We ironed out some wrinkles related to uploaded images on receipts & invoices which should resolve any lingering issues here.

PAX Payment Duplicates

We fixed a rare case where payments would be saved twice to the ticket after processing.
