Invoicing is a feature available to users on our Advanced and Professional plans. You can bill customers for batches of tickets on invoices with a payment period (Net Terms) to indicate when you should be paid for those invoiced goods/services. This allows you to invoice the customer directly, rather than a third-party Billing Agent.

Allow Net Terms Invoicing for a Customer

1. Search for the customer that you wish to assign a Billing Agent and view their Customer Profile.

2. On the right side of the page, click the Edit button to make changes to their profile.

3. On the Edit Customer pop-up, click the Billing tab on the left column. Under the Billing Terms (Net Terms) section, select Has Billing Terms and assign a Billing Cycle. These are required for invoicing the customer. After making your selections, click the Save button to finalize your changes.

  • Has Billing Terms - If selected, this will allow you to create invoices for the customer. De-select to remove this option.

  • Billing Cycle in Days - Enter the billing term (cycle) in days for when the invoice will be due from the customer.

  • Prefers Batch Invoices - If selected, the default when creating an invoice will be to send a single batch invoice for all tickets listed. If not selected, the default will be to invoice each ticket separately with sequential invoice numbers. Invoice email notifications can be set up under Automated Notifications.
