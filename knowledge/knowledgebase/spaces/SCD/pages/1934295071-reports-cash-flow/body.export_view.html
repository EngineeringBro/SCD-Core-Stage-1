The Cash Flow Summary report allows you to look at your transaction (payment) history, grouped by payment method, for a period of time. You can sort by the date when the transaction was added to a ticket or by the date when the ticket status was updated.

Filtering your report by tickets in Closed status allows you to look at only tickets that have been completely closed. This allows you to look at the difference between tickets that are still in open status with pending payments and tickets that have been closed with reconciled payments.

Viewing the Cash Flow Summary Report

On the Navigation Bar, go to Reports. On the Reports page, click on the Accounting dropdown along the top row of report categories. From the available list, click Cash Flow Summary.

Note: If you want to specific details about your cash flow, click Cash Flow Detail.

Use the available filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters. Once you have selected your filters, click Apply Filters to run the report.

  • Date Range - Select a range of dates based on the date a payment was recorded on a ticket.

  • Location - Select a specific location or run an aggregate report for multiple stores.

  • Ticket Date - Select a range of dates based on the last status change of a ticket. This date is subject to change until the ticket is in Closed status.

  • Status - Select whether to scope your results to all tickets, only open tickets, or only closed tickets.

  • Billing Agent - Select to filter by a specific Billing Agent, all agents, or no agents.

If you would like to change views between the Cash Flow Summary and Cash Flow Detail Report, click the link on the upper-right side of the page.

(Optional) Click Hide/Show Filters on the upper-right side of the page to collapse/expand the filters when viewing reports. Click To Excel on the upper-right side of the report page to export an Excel-friendly document. The export will include any filtered data when running the report. 

Cash Flow Summary Report

The Cash Flow Summary Report lists transaction totals, grouped by payment method. You can view all transactions (receipts), payouts, and net receipts. Totals for each column can be found at the bottom of the list.

Cash Flow Detail Report

The Cash Flow Detail report follows the same logic as the Cash Flow Summary Report, but it displays a historical breakdown of each ticket where the payment was recorded. There are additional details that include transaction details— date/time of the transaction, which terminal recorded the transaction, and the staff member who processed the transaction. Each line also has a link to view the ticket with the corresponding transaction.
