Qpop is a customer-facing application designed to enhance interaction and engagement at the point of sale through a dedicated touchscreen device (tablet). Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by:

  • Displaying in-store advertisements and promotions

  • Showing real-time ticket details for customer review

  • Presenting electronic forms and waivers for acknowledgment

  • Capturing electronic signatures for payments via integrated devices

As a browser-based solution, Qpop eliminates the need for expensive proprietary hardware. Simply choose your preferred tablet or touchscreen device, pair it with your location, and start engaging with your customers!

Add a Qpop™ Companion to RepairQ

Log into your RepairQ location on your computer. On the RepairQ Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings Overview page, the left column lists different settings categories. Click on Qpop to expand the options and select Qpop Companions.

Note: Users with the Shift Lead role or higher can pair and launch Qpop.

Create a New Companion

On the Qpop Companions page, click Set up a Qpop Companion. Give your companion a name (without any special characters). Select the location where this companion will be used, and enter a description to add any specific details about this device.

If you would like to add your own images to your Qpop Companion, click the Idle Screen Image to edit the image that will show on the companion when idle. 

Add Images to Your Device

Drag and drop images from your computer to the Image Upload Box for graphics that you wish to upload. You can also click the box to browse files on your computer. For best results, see Qpop™ Companion - Recommended Image Resolutions for sizing your images correctly.

When you upload an image, the preview will show on the companion screen in RepairQ.

Alternatively, you can also click Add from library to select an image that has been previously uploaded to your Qpop Image Library. The Idle-Screen pop-up will allow you to select an image with a preview. Click Add Image to assign the image to your Qpop Companion.

Note: You can upload up to five images which will cycle when Qpop is idle.

Click the Ticket Summary Image to edit the image that will show when you are viewing a ticket.

Drag and drop images from your computer to the Image Upload Box for graphics that you wish to upload. You can also click the box to browse files on your computer or click Add from library to select a previously uploaded image. When you select an image, the preview will show on the companion screen in RepairQ.

Once satisfied with your changes, click Save to finish creating your companion. Your companion device will show on the device list. If you need to change the settings for your Companion, you can click the Edit button to make any changes to your device's name, assignment, or images, and be sure to save after changes.
