Cycle counts are targeted inventory checks that focus on specific items or groups of items. Users with a Technician role or higher can quickly initiate a cycle count, allowing them to inventory selected items without a count assignment. This method is ideal for efficiently managing the stock of one or a few Item Types and is not recommended for an entire store count. Once the counts are submitted, an Inventory Manager or higher is responsible for reviewing the results, reconciling any discrepancies, and making necessary adjustments before finalizing the submission.

  • Efficient Inventory Management - Quickly check stock levels for specific items without a full inventory audit.

  • Accurate Reconciliation - Ensure inventory records are up-to-date by allowing Inventory Managers to review and adjust counts before finalizing.

  • Flexibility - Initiate counts on-demand, whenever needed, without prior scheduling or assignments.

Initiate a Cycle Count

On the RepairQ Navigation Bar, go to Inventory → Inventory Counts. On the Inventory Count Summary Page, click the Count button (eye icon) on the right side of the page to access the Inventory Count Sheet.

Inventory Count Sheet

There are two ways to add items to your Counted Items list: Scan Mode or Lookup Mode. You can toggle between modes by clicking the Search Type icon next to the search field.

Scan Mode

Use Scan Mode to scan SKU/UPC/Serial Number barcodes. When a barcode is scanned or entered, it is automatically added to the Counted Items list.

Lookup Mode

Use Lookup Mode to search for items by their keyword, SKU, or serial number to add the inventory item to the Current Item section of the Count Sheet to record your counted total.

As items are added to the Count Sheet, three sections are updated: The Current Item section, the Counted Items list, and the Count Log.

Current Item

This section shows the most recent scanned/searched item, allowing you to adjust the count total or view counted serial numbers.

  • If the Current Item is non-serialized, you can update the count total to match the found amount. Click Save Count to update the total for the counted Items list or click Cancel to cancel your action for the searched item.

  • If the Current Item is serialized, you can view a list of all counted serial numbers so far. Once you have finished entering serial numbers for this item from the search field, click Save Count to update the Counted Items list or click Cancel to cancel your action for the scanned item.

Counted Items

This shows a list of counted items, their current count total, and available actions.

  • View Serials - View a list of serial numbers counted for this item.

  • Edit - This assigns this item as the active Current Item so you can adjust the count total.

  • Remove - Removes the item from the Counted Items list.

  • Save for Later - Saves your current progress 

  • Save & Submit - Save your progress to submit later, or submit the current count of items from the Counted Item list. Submitting the list will move these items into "Counted" status, ready for a Manager to make adjustments and reconcile the count.

Count Log

This is a log of scanned entries, successful counts, warnings, error messages, etc. Warnings are highlighted in yellow, and errors are highlighted in red. Continue counting your inventory by scanning or adding items to the list.

Saving and Submitting Your Count

If you need to save your counted items, click Save for Later. At a later time, you can pick up where you left off from the same terminal. For more details, check out Inventory Count Process: Pausing, Reassigning, and Deleting Counts.

If you are finished counting all items, click Save & Submit. This will progress the counted items to Counted status. Counted totals are only visible by an Inventory Manager role or higher and are ready for reconciliation.
