Customers may request a refund for an item or service they have already paid for, even though the ticket has not yet been closed. This article will guide you through issuing a refund while ensuring that any refunded items are correctly returned to inventory.

Process a Refund on an Open Ticket

A VCT must be assigned to the terminal to perform a refund on a ticket.

Search for the open ticket in your Ticket Queue. On the Ticket View page, click the Edit Button along the row of actions on the upper-right section of the page.

Remove and Restock the Ticket Item(s)

In the Ticket Items section of the ticket, remove the appropriate item(s) by clicking the Remove Button (X) to the right of the item. A Remove Ticket Item pop-up will appear. Select Restock Immediately, and click Remove.

The Ticket Totals section will now show a pending refund amount.

Refund the Transaction

On the Transaction section of the ticket, click the Refund Button under the Actions column.

Note: Alternatively, you can click the Void Transaction Button next to the Return Button to reverse the transaction in full. If refunding a partial amount, use the Add Transaction Button on the left side of the section.

An Add Transaction pop-up will appear. Select the refund payment method and enter the refund amount as a positive value. Once you have entered the information and additional notes, click Submit Refund.

Note: A refund can be performed for Integrated Payments without the card being present.

Verify the transaction as posted to the ticket and that the payment has been issued back to the customer. Once the balance due is $0.00, you can Close the ticket to finalize the process.
