Release 11/02/2018 12:00PM CT (17:00 UTC)

Fixed Electronic Signatures on Windows 10

With the Windows update on October 30, our solution for electronic signatures stopped working when using a touch screen in the Edge and Chrome browsers. When attempting to sign with your finger, the page would scroll rather than draw a signature. With this update the issue is resolved, and all methods of signing (mouse, touchpad, touchscreen) are working normally.
