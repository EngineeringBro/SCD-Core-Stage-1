Released 02/13/2018 CT 12:07pm CT (18:07 UTC)

Purchase Order Item List

We have condensed the purchase order item list to fit better on smaller screens. Specifically, we removed the SKU and UPC columns and added them as sub-lines in the catalog item column.

Ticket Item Default Qty

Inventoried items now always get a default qty of 1 when they are added to a ticket.

Qsite

By popular demand, the staff member's last name has been removed from ticket comments.

Sales Referral Summary Report Download

We added a new excel download for the Sales Referral Summary.

Announcement Deleting

We fixed an issue that would prevent deleting announcements

Inventory Lock for Backordered Items

Inventory locks are now pro-actively released on ticket save so that the inventory items are editable afterwards (when in a non-consumed status).
