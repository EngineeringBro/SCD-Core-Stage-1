Released on 6/9/2021 at 11:30pm CT

Enhancements

  • The Customer List can now be filtered by the date customers were created.

  • Ticket Barcodes on receipts can now appear at the top or the bottom of the receipt. You can choose where you want the barcode to appear through receipt print settings.

Bug Fixes

  • Unicode characters no longer cause RepairQ to crash and log the user out when entered into search fields.

  • A bug that caused RepairQ to scroll up to the top of the page when waiving a charge or trying to add bundled items has been fixed.

  • A customer can no longer have another customer listed as their referral, if that customer already has the other customer listed as a referral. In other words, Customer A cannot have Customer B as a referral, if Customer B already has Customer A as a referral. This had previously been allowed as an oversight, and would cause an error within the system.

  • Ticket notes no longer extend horizontally, and now wrap text to avoid having the user scroll back and forth while typing.

  • A bug preventing custom Ticket Queue list views from exporting data beyond 90 days has been fixed.

  • Serial numbers and passwords can now be removed from customer repair devices. Previously, attempting to remove this info would not be successful after saving.

  • The Inactive Attribute filter now excludes all Active items from the Catalog list view.

  • Inventory managers can now properly see incoming internal transfer requests, no longer requiring a role at the order's originating location.

  • A bug that allowed refurbishment tickets, created from laway trade-in tickets, to update inventory items in the Sold status has been resolved.

  • Users with the Accountant role can now properly access the export options under list views
