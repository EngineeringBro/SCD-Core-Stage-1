Full Release: December 28, 2016

New Payment Device Tech

RepairQ 1.9 features the new integrated EMV enabled payment device, the PAX S300. The PAX S300 is both P2PE and EMV enabled today, and the device has the ability to read chip cards, magnetic strips, and NFC. The PAX S300 also has a touch screen for capturing electronic signatures. There is a lot of technology packed into this device, and it has the ability to receive updates from Bluefin to add additional payment methods in the future like Samsung Pay, Apple Pay, and other NFC driven payment technologies.
