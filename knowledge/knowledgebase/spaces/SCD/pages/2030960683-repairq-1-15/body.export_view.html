Opportunities Management Suite

With 1.15, RepairQ introduces a powerful CRM-inspired toolkit to create, track, and convert sales opportunities into happy, loyal customers. This release will be one of several to add new tools to what we’re simply calling our Opportunities Management Suite

Contacts

Tracking relationships is essential in developing and maintaining your sales process. In the past, RepairQ users could only track one or two structured types of relationships, like a Customer. Now with Contacts, you will be able to create and track a whole new type of relationship, assign custom tags to them (like lead or prospect), attach quotes and appointments to them, and convert them to a Customer after their first sale. Contacts, just like Customers, can be searched, attached, tracked, and reported on.

Quotes

With our next major release, you will be able to create quotes for customers and leads, print or email them, and then convert quotes into tickets after they are accepted. A quote has all of the features of a “regular” sale/repair/trade-in ticket with a few minor differences:

  1. Inventory is not consumed.

  1. Quotes are not included in revenue reports.

Converting a quote to a working ticket is a one-click process; and inventory stock is pulled at the time of conversion. Quotes can have their own workflows, leveraging the ticket status system to track progress, and the automated notification rules can be configured specifically for quotes.

Appointments

“When would you like to come in?” Now with appointment scheduling being built into RepairQ, you can book appointments for customers and leads, create new tickets from appointments, and track your upcoming appointments on an easily accessible calendar view.

Appointments are created with the customer’s request and device information so that when the customer checks in for their appointment, creating their ticket for service is a push-button process. You can also create a new lead record in the process of booking an appointment, so if the appointment does not convert, you retain the customer’s information and device info in the leads list so that you can perform targeted marketing.

Appointment notifications are configurable, including appointment reminders, and you can keep track of appointment statuses and resolutions with the customizable status system.

Managing your appointment calendar rules is highly customizable, and it honors your store hours, holidays, time slot durations, concurrent appointments allowed, and more.

Accounting Journal Improvements

Until now, inventory was only visible on the Journal Entry when it was consumed. You are now able to pull Journal Entries that include all inventory item add, delete, and updates (instead of just when consumed on tickets). To support this behavior, inventory items sold on tickets are now kept in the "pulled" status until the ticket is closed, at which point they move to the "sold" status.

Other Features

Pricing Plan Changes + Billing Page Updates

We are currently restructuring our pricing plans. Our goal is to always empower our users, and this is no exception. We will soon be offering three simple plans that each effectively fill industry needs for every stage of business. Users will now be included in the base price. We are also adding in options for annual pricing and special incentives that will save you money over time (up to 20% off).

Tax Inclusive Pricing

Tax inclusive pricing allows you to display price including tax on labels, receipts, etc. and was added to help countries like Australia communicate a tax-inclusive price with their customers.

Warranty Life Bundle Offers

Through our integration with Warranty Life, you can now offer three new bundle offers to your customers and you can display and/or promote them directly through Qpop with the latest release!
