RepairQ’s tax-inclusive pricing feature is tailored for businesses operating in regions where local legislation mandates managing and displaying prices, including taxes. This ensures a seamless and transparent pricing experience for customers.

In markets like Australia, where Goods and Services Tax (GST) applies, RepairQ can be configured to manage tax-inclusive pricing in compliance with regulations. This feature ensures that unit pricing, discounts, and ticket totals include tax, while accurately calculating the base price for gross profit analysis and providing a detailed tax breakdown for customer invoicing and accounting.

Key Features:

  • Catalog Price Including Tax - The catalog will convert the unit price to ‘price including tax’. This will enable users to enter and manage a single catalog unit price including tax.

  • Price and Tax Breakdown - RepairQ automatically calculates the base price and tax breakdown for invoicing and reporting of gross profits.

  • Discount Management - Discounts, whether fixed prices or percentages can be applied, including tax.

  • Customer Invoices & Receipts - Customer paperwork can be configured to show tax-inclusive pricing and tax paid per unit.

  • Display Layout - Configure default labels for price, including tax, across key features such as tickets and lists.
