Released on: 9/13/16 1:00 CT

This is a minor release with a few bug fixes.

Invoice Remittance

Tickets linked to an invoice that are removed from the invoice will no longer be updated with future invoice updates. In addition, the remittance invoice item filters will filter the items appropriately once again.

Location Leaderboard Permissions

The location leaderboard will now only show the locations where a user is the manager (or admin).

Accountant Role Permissions

Give the accountant role permission to view customer data by default.

Ticket History, load on demand

To improve page load times, we no longer load the history with every ticket view or edit screen. Instead, a "load history" button is present where you may load the ticket history only when you need it.
