When adding inventoried items to a ticket, RepairQ pulls them as individual line items rather than allowing a quantity adjustment. While non-inventory items—such as labor, services, or items without individual stock tracking—can have their quantity adjusted freely, inventoried items must be added as separate line items to maintain to track inventory movement (Instock → Pulled → Sold). We’ve streamlined this process by providing a way to adding multiple quantites of the same inventoried item to a ticket. This feature is especially useful for high-volume sales or repairs requiring multiple identical parts.

Note: For best performance, keep the total number of line items per ticket to 75 or fewer.

Add Multiple Inventoried Items to a Ticket

When creating or editing a ticket, click the Look Up button instead of using the Smart Search box. In the Add Ticket Item pop-up, use Advanced Search to find the catalog item.

In the Add Ticket Item pop-up, use the search fields to find the item, add the quantity to add to the ticket, and click Add Item.

Note: For best performance, keep the total number of line items per ticket to 75 or fewer.

The items are added to your ticket as individual line items in Pulled status. To remove extra items, click the Delete button (red X) to return them to Instock status.
