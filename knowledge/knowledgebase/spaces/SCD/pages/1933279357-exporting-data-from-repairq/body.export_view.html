In RepairQ, managers can download export files in Excel format. These exports are invaluable for reviewing your business details and maintaining accurate records. You can export a variety of lists and reports tailored to your needs.

Maximize Your List Exports

To create specific lists and reports, you can customize your list data by applying filters. Please note that the exported data will be limited to any filters you set prior to the export. To optimize performance, consider filtering your results for a smaller, more specific export instead of attempting a large one. Exports that are deemed "too large" often involve multiple locations and numerous catalog items.

Import Note on Imports

If you also use imports to create or update data within RepairQ, please be aware that exported files are different from import templates. While exporting lists and reports is intended for historical records, these exported files cannot be used as import templates due to differing column data requirements.

Exporting Data from a List

When viewing a List, the Action Button is often seen at the upper-right of the table. Export buttons for Tickets, Customers, Inventory, and Catalog pages can be found in the Action Menu. Lists can also be customized to include various fields. Only visible columns will show on the export. For more information on customing your List View, see List Views: Editing Columns.

Exporting Data from a Report

When viewing a Report, the To Excel button is often at the upper-right side of the page.

Additionally, some reports will also have the To Excel button at the bottom of the table.
