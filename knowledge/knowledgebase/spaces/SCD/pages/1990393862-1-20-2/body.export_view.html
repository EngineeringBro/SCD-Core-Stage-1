Improvements

  • Bluefin Integration now allows debit transactions. Depending on the card type, customers can now choose whether to pay with debit or credit networks.

  • Improvements to Integrated Payments, reducing duplicate transactions with identical Transaction IDs on the same ticket.

  • Improvements to tax-inclusive pricing for GST compliance.

Bug Fixes

  • Appointment concurrency settings no longer conflict with closed appointments.

  • Activating availability windows will not interfere with appointment visibility in the list view.

  • Optimized reporting to generate results when selecting more than 150 locations.

  • Tip transaction details are no longer hidden after generating a Reconciliation Report.

  • Tip refund logic has been fixed when selecting "Refund Tip Amount First".

  • Integrated transactions without a tip will no longer appear on the Tip Report.

  • Integrated partial payments no longer incorrectly show as the full amount paid on a ticket.

  • Existing quantities on a Purchase Order are now unaffected after using the Quick Create feature while creating the PO.
