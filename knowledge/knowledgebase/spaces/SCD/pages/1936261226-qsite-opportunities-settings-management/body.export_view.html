Accessing Qsite Opportunities Settings

Users with the Admin role can access these settings. On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings â†’ Company Settings.

On the Settings Overview page, scroll down to the Qsite section. The features of each setting are outlined below.

Embed

This page includes options for using Qsite through your website. There are available options for Qsite Ticket Status, Qsite Opportunities, and Qsite Ticket Lookup. For more information on this process, see Embed Qsiteâ„˘ Features Into Your Website.

General Settings

This page includes options to customize your logo and color theme based on your brand, Navigation Breadcrumb settings, Terms of Use disclaimers, and Support Language to notify visitors when encountering an unexpected error.

Locations

This page includes Location Finder settings for searching your locations on a map. You can select a search range and location limit, and customize the message displayed to customers when searching for a location. You can also enable/disable locations from search visibility on the Qsite Opportunities page.

Request Types

This page is where you configure the services offered on the Qsite Opportunities page. Before using this feature, you must configure your Category Tree, link catalog items to the categories, and (optionally) configure Item compatibility.

Determine whether you would like to offer Repair services to your customers on Qsite. If enabled, you can select which Item Grid to use for navigation and select compatible services.

Contact Form

On this page, you can configure the message to customers when they are prompted to submit their contact information.

Quotes

This page is used to configure Quote price visibility, including the Advertised Price reflected in the item's Catalog settings.

You can also configure the message to customers after they submit their contact information and view their quotes.

There are also settings to change the message when quotes are disabled, the price is not advertised, or the price cannot be identified.

Ticket

This page allows you to configure the title and description on the Quote confirmation page, the Ticket Lookup page, and Ticket Resolution requirements when canceling a quote.

The Ticket Lookup feature works with the Qsite Embed feature to provide customers with a place to manage their ticket and appointment details.

Appointments

This page allows you to enable/disable customer-level appointment scheduling from Qsite, manage appointment block scheduling, and customize the message for customers when choosing an appointment time and when receiving their appointment confirmation. You can also select the default appointment status that will be applied to appointments updated via Qsite.

Ticket Approval

If you use the Ticket Approval feature, this page will allow you to enable/disable an online portal where customers can view approval requests and accept/reject them electronically. You can customize the message for customers when requesting authorization and the confirmation page for when they approve/decline the approval.

You can add supplemental terms and conditions for approvals.

You can also require the customer to view the approval document before submitting approval and require them to leave a note upon submission.
