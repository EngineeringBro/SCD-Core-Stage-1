Improvements and Bug Fixes

  • Inventory Details List View - You can now add the columns “Serial Number”, “Price”, “Cost”, and “Average Cost” to the Inventory Details List View. These columns display the serial number (if any), price, cost, and average cost of the inventory item at your location. Note: The “Price” and “Cost” columns may display different values than the “Price | Cost” column, which displays an item’s catalog price, not the price of the actual inventory item which may have location overrides applied.

  • We fixed a bug where adding, editing, or removing a supplier SKUs from a catalog item did not display the update in the change log at all.

  • We fixed a bug where sometimes users would experience an error when trying to send a ticket approval email to a customer.
