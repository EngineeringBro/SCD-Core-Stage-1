1.17.11 is a minor update including some small features and bug fixes.

Scheduled for 11:30 CT on Aug 10, 2020

Enhancements and Bug Fixes

  • Updated the ordering of ticket items on printed receipts so that child items in a bundle are always shown together with their parent, regardless of when the child item was added to the ticket.

  • Added the ability to more effectively delete holidays in settings.

  • Fixed a bug with handling of payment declines where electronic signatures are captured.

  • Adjusted the status flow for backorders to enable the following transitions:

    • Pending Notifiction to In Repair

    • Pending Notification to Repair Completed

    • On Hold to Repair Completed
