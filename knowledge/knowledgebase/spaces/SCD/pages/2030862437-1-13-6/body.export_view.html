Released 10/03/17 2:08 CT (19:08 UTC)

Ticket Notes in Notifications

A new shortcode is available for automated notifications to include customer facing (printable) notes in ticket notifications.

Custom Form Order

The sort order of custom forms on tickets now sorts based on the order number from your custom form settings.

Cash Reconciliation Print - page breaks

The forced page break before the "notes" section has been removed. This will reduce the number of pages printed.

Timesheets with duplicate staff names

We fixed an issue with grouping and displaying results where two staff have the exact same first and last name.

Onboarding Videos

We added direct access to the onboarding videos from the dashboard.
