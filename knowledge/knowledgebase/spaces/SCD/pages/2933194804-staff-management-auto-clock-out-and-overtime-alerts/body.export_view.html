Accurate time tracking ensures you have a clear view of labor costs, employee hours, and overtime, helping you manage your team efficiently while keeping payroll accurate. RepairQ simplifies attendance management with built-in tools for clocking in and out, adding or editing time records, and monitoring overtime with helpful notifications. With these features, you can focus on running your business while ensuring your staff's time is accounted for.

  • Clock-In and Clock-Out - Enable timesheet functionality to allow staff members to record their work hours directly within RepairQ. This feature ensures accurate tracking of hours worked and simplifies payroll management.

  • Overtime Management and Alerts - Define a daily threshold for clocked-in hours and receive a dashboard notification to monitor potential overtime, manage labor costs, and address discrepancies.

  • Auto Clock-Out Settings - Set a specific time for automatic clock-out. Staff members who remain clocked in beyond this time will be logged out automatically.

Enable Timesheet Records for a Staff Profile

When editing a user’s Staff Profile, selecting Timesheet Required will enable the option for the user to clock in and out of RepairQ. While it is not mandatory for them to clock in before working, failing to do so will result in their hours not being properly logged.

Overtime Alerts and Auto Clock Out

To view and manage store details, including overtime alerts and automatic clock-out settings, navigate to Location Settings → Location Info. Here, you can configure the following:

  • Staff Overtime Hours - Set the threshold for staff clocked-in hours per day. If a staff member exceeds this limit, you will receive an alert on the Dashboard.

  • Automatic Clock Out - Set a time for automatic clock-out. Any staff member still clocked in at this time will be automatically clocked out for the day.

  • Week Start Day - Choose the starting day of the week for viewing attendance and weekly calendar reports, ensuring consistency across all schedules.

Overtime Dashboard Alert

The Overtime Dashboard Alert notifies you when an employee exceeds the overtime threshold for the day. It provides a breakdown of the employee’s name, logged time, and the date of the overtime. A link to the Attendance page is also included for easy access to make corrections.
