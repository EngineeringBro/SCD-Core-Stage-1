Released Deployed on 9/22/16 11:12 CT

This is a minor release with a few bug fixes.

Staff "Status" Filter

The "Status" filter was not being applied to the "unassigned" group of staff members listed on the "Name" tab of the staff index. The "Status" filter will now filter the results for that group accordingly.

Summary Report

Fixed an issue with the Summary Report when performing monthly rollups spanning multiple years of data where the  "February"  header was skipped for 2016.

Removing Ticket Items

When removing inventory items on a ticket, the "void" status option will no longer be present for non-managers, unless the item is in the "backordered" status.
