A ticket note is a great way to communicate an important memo between staff members. RepairQ makes it easy to attach special notes or instructions to any ticket and they can be flagged as important to increase visibility for other staff members who view the ticket. Notes can also be shared with customers on their receipts and when viewing their ticket status on Qsite. Notes can include special formatting, embedded links, and images.

Adding a Note to a Ticket

1. Create a new ticket or Searching for Tickets to which you would like to add a note.

2. On the Ticket View page, scroll to the Notes section at the bottom of the page. You can either click on Add Note or select the Add (+) button to create a new note.

3. An Add Note window will appear. Click inside the text field to enter your note. The top row of formatting options allows you to change the appearance of the text, add bullets/lists, and attach links and images to your note. The selections available below the note field allow you to change the visibility of the note.

  • Visible to customer - Selecting this option makes your note visible on receipts and when the customer checks their ticket status on Qsite.

  • Flag as important - Selecting this option will make your note appear at the top of the ticket, highlighted in yellow. This will improve the visibility of the note for other users who view the ticket.

4. Once you have entered your note and any optional images or links, click Add Note to save your changes.

Note: When attaching an image to a note, you cannot use local images. You will need to provide the source URL for the image. Our recommendation is to upload that image to an image-sharing website, then attach the linked URL to the note.

5. The note will now appear in the Notes section of the ticket. Under the Actions column, there are additional options for the note.

  • Flag as Important - The hazard symbol will toggle the note as important (yellow). If you would like to remove the flagged note, you can click the symbol to remove this flag (blue).

  • Visible to Customer - The person symbol will toggle the note as visible to the customer on receipts and Qsite (yellow). If you would like to hide the note, click the symbol again to make the note visible to staff only (blue).

  • Delete - The red X symbol will delete the note. Staff with Admin or Location Manager roles can delete notes.

Note: If you would like to edit a note, you must delete the note and re-add the corrected message.
