When a Virtual Cash Terminal (VCT) is closed, staff will fill out a form to compare their current cash drawer balance to the transactions/cash transfers applied while the drawer was open. This allows staff to see any variance in cash flow. The Cash Reconciliation Report page allows you to view all of your past (VCT) reconciliation sheets for a specific time period, along with links to view the report in full detail.

Viewing Cash Reconciliation Report History

On the Navigation Bar, go to Reports. On the Reports page, click on the Cash Reconciliation link under the Cash Reconciliation category.

Use the filters to scope results based on the assigned parameters. Once you have selected your filters, click Apply Filters to run the report.

  • Item Date - Select the date range for the list of Reconciliation Reports. If you want to view a report for a VCT that was closed on the current day, select tomorrow's date for the end date.

  • Location - Select a specific location or run an aggregate report for multiple stores.

  • Date Type - Select to view reports based on their Opened date or Closed date. By default, this field is set to view by Closed date.

(Optional) Click Hide/Show Filters on the upper-right side of the page to collapse/expand the filters when viewing reports.

Cash Reconciliation by Date and Terminal

The Reconciliation Report list gives you details about past reporting, including the Report ID with a link to view the report in full detail, Open/Close dates for the VCT, Terminal Name, Location, Net Cash (the Cash Over/Short amount in the report), and Cash On Hand (the beginning balance when the VCT was assigned).
