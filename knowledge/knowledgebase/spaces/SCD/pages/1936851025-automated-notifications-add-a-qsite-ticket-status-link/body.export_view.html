Qsite Ticket Status provides an easy way for your customers to check their ticket status. The ticket status page shows a summary of the ticket, the current status of the ticket, the balance due, and when it was last updated. Users can send customers a link to Qsite when sending outgoing notifications.

Send an Automated Notification with a Qsite Ticket Status Link

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings â†’ Location Settings.

2. On the Settings Overview screen, click the Communications dropdown on the left column of the list of settings. From the list of options, click Notifications.

3. On the Notification Automation Overview page, you can add a new notification by clicking Create Notification Automation or add this link to an existing notification by clicking the Edit button under the Actions column.

4. For this example, we are creating a new notification. On the New Automation Notification screen, begin filling out applicable fields.

  • Notification Name - Give your Automated Notification a name to identify its purpose.

  • Location - Select whether this notification is strictly for your location or all locations (if applicable).

  • Trigger - Qsite status shortcodes are available when selecting either Ticket Notification or Ticket Status Change.

    • Ticket Notification - The notification is sent out manually when clicking the notification button on the ticket. You can choose whether to send it via email or SMS.

    • Ticket Status Change - The notification is automatically queued for delivery when the ticket is placed into the assigned status. Select the ticket status based on your preference for delivery timing.

  • Delivery - Select whether this notification will be delivered to the customer's email or via SMS.

    • For SMS, the customer will need an assigned SMS Phone number and the appropriate carrier.

Notification Short Codes

Short Codes are dynamic values that change based on the context of your notification. Qsite Short Codes are available for any notification when selecting the triggers for Ticket Notification or Ticket Status Change.

After making your selections, click Save to update your changes and create the notification.

Message Subject

Create a subject for your notification, similar to the subject of an email. You can enter any of the available shortcodes above to customize the available information.

Send To

Copy/paste {customer} into this field, so the system knows this notification will be delivered to the customer assigned to the ticket.

SMS Message

Add the message that will be communicated to the customer. To include a link to check their ticket status, make sure you include {ticket_status_url} somewhere in the message. This link will allow the customer to check their ticket status, plus additional information including balance due, customer-visible notes, and your store's contact information.Â
