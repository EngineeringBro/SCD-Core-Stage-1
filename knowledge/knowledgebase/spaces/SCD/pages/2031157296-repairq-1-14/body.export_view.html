Enterprise Management

Managing large organizations is now much easier, and you can configure specific settings for local and regional differences.

Settings Localization

Nearly all settings now have two layers, company-wide, and location specific. Now you can more effectively manage the differences between your company-wide standards and your local or regional location differences.

Settings that can now be defined per location are as follows:

  • Referral Sources

  • Ticket Custom Forms

  • Ticket Status Labels

  • Ticket Resolutions

  • Warranty Claims

  • Invoicing labels and settings

  • Shippers

  • Payment Methods

  • Tax Groups

  • Tax Classes

  • Manufacturers

  • Suppliers

  • Purchase Order Status Labels

  • Miscellaneous Settings (some of them)

Catalog Localization

Enforcing company wide standards, while allowing for customization or local differences is now easier than ever. New location overrides are available to help you standardize your company catalog but allow for differing location pricing, costs, identifiers, and more. In addition, you can now create catalog items that are scoped to one or more specific locations. No more cluttering up your catalog with duplicate or misnamed items. Now you have the ability to enforce your company-wide standards while giving the right amount of control to individual managers and store owners.

New Staff Roles

Two new staff roles were introduced, and one role changed names.

"Manager" is now "Location Admin"

The Manager role is now called a "Location Admin." This user has control over all of the settings that are specific to the location(s) they manage. This role is appropriate for store owners and administrators.

New "Manager" Role

This new role is designed to separate the "Location Admin" from a classic "Store Manager." The manager role has elevated to perform the kinds of tasks required of an in-store manager, but they cannot change most settings.

New "Location Advisor" Role

This new role is designed to give read-only access to given stores in the same capacity as the "Accountant" super-role. The "Location Advisor" can view reports, lists, history, etc, but they cannot edit or delete anything.

Switch Location Feature

In the past, too many clicks were required to login to a different location. You can now switch locations much easier for PO creation and receiving, inventory adjustments, and time tracking. The new “Switch Location” box will only show you the locations in which you have an active role, making the list much smaller for large organizations.
