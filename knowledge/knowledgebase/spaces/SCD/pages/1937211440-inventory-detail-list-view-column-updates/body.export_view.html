With RepairQ 1.18.13, we have introduced separate columns for previously merged information found on the Inventory Detail page. This provides users with more customization when viewing inventory items and sorting that information when exporting data.

Below is a list of the columns that can now be separated:

  • SKU | SN

  • Status | Condition

  • Price | Cost

Note: The Price and Cost columns may display different values than the Price | Cost column, which displays an item’s catalog price, not the price of the actual inventory item which may have location overrides applied.

Adding New Columns to the Inventory Detail List

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Inventory → Inventory Detail.

2. On the Inventory Detail screen, click the Options button (⋮) on the right side of the page. From the dropdown list of options, select Edit Columns.

3-1. Click the Add Button (+), and the Column Edit window will appear. These categories are the current columns on your catalog page.

3-2. Scroll down on the pop-up until you reach the Add Columns section. This section contains all of the fields that you can add to your Inventory Detail view. Select the checkbox next to the field(s) you would like to add to the Column List. Additionally, you can uncheck the box next to any field to remove it from the Column List.

Alternatively, you can also search for your desired column(s) by typing a keyword into the category box (For example, cost). The pop-up will then display any columns containing that keyword.

3-3. Once you are finished customizing your list, click the Finish button (checkmark) to update your list.

4. After updating the columns, your Inventory Detail list will reflect the new columns added.

Note: You can save this new list view by following the instructions in our Knowledge Base Article, Saving and Sharing Custom List Views.
