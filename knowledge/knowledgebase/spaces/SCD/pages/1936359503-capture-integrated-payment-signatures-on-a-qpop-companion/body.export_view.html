Qpop is a customer-facing application designed to enhance interaction and engagement at the point of sale through a dedicated touchscreen interface. Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by:

  • Displaying in-store advertisements and promotions

  • Showing real-time ticket details for customer review

  • Presenting electronic forms and waivers for acknowledgment

  • Capturing electronic signatures for payments via integrated devices

Qpop Companion works with Bluefin integrated payment systems, allowing customers to sign for their transactions on the companion device.

Send Payment Signatures to Qpop

On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings Overview page, there is a list of settings categories in the left column. Click Payments to expand the dropdown and then click Payment Devices.

On the Payment Terminal page, locate the device you want to record signatures with Qpop. Under the Actions column, click the Edit button. When editing the payment device settings, on the Signature Source field, select Qpop Companion from the dropdown and click the Require Signature box. Once you are satisfied with your changes, click Save.

When requesting Bluefin card payment in RepairQ, the customer will swipe/insert their card into the PAX device as usual. During the payment process, Qpop will prompt the customer for their signature on the tablet. After signing, the customer will tap Done Signing to complete the transaction.
