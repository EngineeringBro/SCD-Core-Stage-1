Qpop is a customer-facing application designed to enhance interaction and engagement at the point of sale through a dedicated touchscreen interface. Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by:

  • Displaying in-store advertisements and promotions

  • Showing real-time ticket details for customer review

  • Presenting electronic forms and waivers for acknowledgment

  • Capturing electronic signatures for payments via integrated devices

Once you add a Qpop Companion to your RepairQ location, you must pair the device to a terminal. A companion can be paired with multiple terminals at the same time.

Note: Qpop Companions are intended to run as Always-On status for both the screen and internet connection. Most tablets will disconnect from Wi-Fi when the screen times out or goes into sleep mode. When running Qpop, ensure your tablet is plugged in and screen timeout and auto-sleep settings have been turned off. We also recommend enabling Kiosk Mode on your tablet first to ensure that customers cannot navigate away from the Qpop browser page.

Pair Qpop With Your Terminal

On Your Computer/Terminal

Navigate to your RepairQ URL and log in to your location. On the Navigation Bar, click the Location Shortname dropdown. From the list of options, click Pair Qpop.

On the Qpop Companion pop-up, select which companion you would like to use from the dropdown list. Once you have made your selection, click Pair. The pop-up will display generate a code. Keep this screen open while you log into your Qpop tablet.

On Your Qpop Companion Device

Navigate to your RepairQ URL and log in to your location. Under the Get Started section of the dashboard, click Launch Qpop. You will be directed to the Welcome to Qpop page. Enter the code that was generated by RepairQ on your computer and tap Pair.

Pairing Confirmation

When paired, your computer will display an image, showing that the companion has paired successfully and your Companion will show the Idle Screen.

Note: If the pairing was unsuccessful or you need to generate a new pairing code, you can click Get New Code on the popup on your computer.

Optional: Add a Qpop Companion Link to Your Home Screen

iPadOS

When viewing the idle screen on your browser, tap Share on the toolbar. Then, tap Add to Home Screen. Name your link something that is easy to identify, then tap Add.

Your home screen will now have a link directly to your Qpop Companion page.

Android

When viewing the idle screen on your browser, tap Options on your toolbar. On the list of options, tap Add to Home Screen. Name your link something that is easy to identify, then tap Add.

Your home screen will now have a link to your Qpop Companion page.
