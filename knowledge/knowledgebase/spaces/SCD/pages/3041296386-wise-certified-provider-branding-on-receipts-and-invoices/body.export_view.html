Show your commitment to service excellence by adding the WISE Certified logo to your customer receipts and invoices. The WISE Authorized Service Provider (ASP) designation signifies that your store meets industry-recognized standards for high-quality repairs on smartphones, tablets, and Chromebooks. To ensure authenticity, RepairQ will validate your WISE certification before enabling the receipt and invoice configuration. Once enabled, you’ll have the flexibility to manually turn this feature off if needed.

  • Differentiate Your Business – Set your store apart from non-certified competitors.

  • Boost Customer Confidence – Encourage repeat business by showcasing industry recognition.

  • Support Brand Reputation – Align your shop with a recognized repair standard.

Activate WISE Certified Integration

On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the settings page, under the Account section, click Integrations.

Under the list of available integrations, click WISE. On the integration page, make sure to Enable WISE Certification Printing, enter your WISE Code, and Save your changes.

Once submitted, RepairQ will reach out to WISE database to validate the ID. Changing the WISE code will verify the code again.

Add WISE Logo to Receipts and Invoices

Navigate to Location Settings and, under the Templates section, select Receipts or Invoices.

On the Template Settings page, select Show WISE Certification Image and Save your changes.
