RepairQ offers integrated payment processing through Bluefin Payment Systems, Heartland Payment Systems, and Square. Bluefin and Heartland integration is currently available for customers in the United States, and Square is available in the United States and Canada.

RepairQ supports Europay, MasterCard, Visa (EMV), and Point-to-Point Encryption (P2PE) payment processing for secure and reliable payment processing. For an introduction to integrated payments, additional resources, and tutorials, start here!

Note: For payment processing with Bluefin or Heartland, you must order and receive your payment terminals directly from our partner. Each device is injected with a key at their provisioning facility and arrives at your location with tamper seals to protect you from insecure or tampered devices. Square Terminal can be purchased from Square, here, or purchased through an authorized retailer.

Supported Bluefin Devices

PAX S300

This device has the ability to read EMV chip cards, NFC, and magnetic strips, and it has a keypad for keyed entry. It is both EMV and P2PE enabled. This device must be connected to the internet, and your point of sale terminal must be able to connect to it over your local network. You must connect it with an ethernet cable to your network. We recommend that you use a wired connection to your router without a network switch.

PAX D210

This device has the ability to read EMV chip cards, NFC, and magnetic strips, and it has a keypad for keyed entry. It is both EMV and P2PE enabled. This device must be connected to your 2.4 GHz WiFi network, and your point of sale terminal must be connected to your local network.

PAX A80/A920

This Android device has the ability to read EMV chip cards, NFC, and magnetic strips, and it has a touch screen for keyed entry. It is both EMV and P2PE enabled. This device can be connected to your 2.4 GHz WiFi network, and your point of sale terminal must be connected to your local network. We recommend that you use a wired connection to your router without a network switch.

IDTECH SREDKey

This device has the ability to read magnetic strips, and it has a keypad for keyed entry. It is P2PE enabled. This device connects to your point-of-sale terminal via a USB port.

IDTECH M130

This device has the ability to read magnetic strips, and it has a keypad for keyed entry. It is P2PE enabled. This device connects to your point-of-sale terminal via a USB port.
