1.17.12 is a minor update including some small features and bug fixes.

11:30 CT on September 16, 2020

Enhancements

  • The Payment Device screens for connection tests are now available to additional roles.

  • Ticket timers have been optimized for faster load times.

  • Uniqueness checks for records that have unique constraints (e.g. no two catalog items allowed with the same name) have been optimized for faster saves.

  • The notification queue now has a location filter, and is visible to location admins.
