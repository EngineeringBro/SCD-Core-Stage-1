RepairQ offers opportunity management for Contacts (leads), Customers, Quotes, and Appointments. Users can easily create quotes that can be printed, emailed, and converted into tickets.

A quote has all of the features of a standard ticket with a few key differences:

  • Inventory is not pulled nor consumed for a quote.

  • Quotes are not included in revenue reports.

  • Quotes do not require a transaction to close out the ticket.

Quotes also use the same status system that you use for tickets and repairs. Converting a quote into an active ticket is a one-click process, and once converted, the related inventory is immediately pulled to the ticket.

Convert a Quote to a Ticket

On the Navigation Bar, go to Opportunities → Quotes. Use the filters to locate the quote that you would like to convert. Next to the quote, click the Options button (⋮) under the Actions column. From the list of options, click Convert.

The quote will convert to a ticket, and any pending inventory will be pulled from stock. You can save the ticket if you need to make changes at a later date or continue with the regular ticket workflow.
