A Contact (lead) is classified as a potential customer for your business. Profiles can be created to collect customer data used for sales, repairs, and marketing communication.  A Contact Profile has all of the data points of a standard Customer Profile with a couple of key differences:

  • Contacts can be viewed separately on the Contacts page.

  • If a ticket with an assigned Contact is completed, the contact is automatically converted to a Customer.

Creating a New Contact Profile

1. On the Navigation Bar, go to Opportunities → +Contact.

2. Alternatively, while on the Contacts Overview page, you can click the Add (+) button on the right side of the page.

3. A Create Contact pop-up window will appear. Begin filling out the contact's information. At a minimum, RepairQ requires the following fields for creating a contact.

  • First Name

  • Last Name

  • Primary Phone

Note: If you are on RepairQ Advanced or RepairQ Professional, Admins can customize the required fields by updating Validation Rules for Customers. If there are additional required fields to create a profile, these are marked with an asterisk ( * ) next to their title.

4. On the left column of the window, there are category tabs for entering secondary information for the customer.

  1. 5. Once you have filled out all required and are satisfied with adding optional information, click the +Create button to save your changes.

Note: When you submit customer details, RepairQ will search your existing customers for a matching entry based on last name and phone number. If one exists, you will be notified with a message with a link to view the existing profile.

6. After creating the contact you will be taken to their Contact Profile page, where you can view an overview of their details. When viewing this page, the left column has additional management categories and the right side has options to edit the profile as well as a quick-create button for starting a new ticket with the customer's information attached to it
