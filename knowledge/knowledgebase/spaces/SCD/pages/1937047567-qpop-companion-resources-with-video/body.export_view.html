What is Qpop Companion?

Qpop is a customer-facing application designed to enhance interaction and engagement at the point of sale through a dedicated touchscreen device (tablet). Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by: Always facing the customer, Qpop allows for seamless interaction during the sales process by:

  • Displaying in-store advertisements and promotions

  • Showing real-time ticket details for customer review

  • Presenting electronic forms and waivers for acknowledgment

  • Capturing electronic signatures for payments via integrated devices

As a browser-based solution, Qpop eliminates the need for expensive proprietary hardware. Simply choose your preferred tablet or touchscreen device, pair it with your location, and start engaging with your customers!
