Released 03/07/2017 at 10:47 CT (16:47 UTC)

Ticket Form Signatures

Signature previews would not always display properly for unsaved forms, although they would be saved and presented properly after save. To prevent any confusion, we made a few adjustments to how signatures are previewed for saved forms.

Analytics Index

The analytics index page received some updates to reflect the new language describing the Business Intelligence Suite features.

Inventory Label Images

The maximum image size is now constrained to 3 inches wide.

Supplier Overrides

For items backordered on a ticket and ordered through a PO, the supplier record could get cleared out when re-saving the ticket. This issue has been resolved.

Inventory Expense Report

The shipping cost was not adjusting based on the inventory date filters set on the Inventory Expense Report. The shipping cost will now obey the filters appropriately.
