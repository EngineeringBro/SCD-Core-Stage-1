The Inventory Count process helps ensure that the quantities tracked in RepairQ match the actual stock on hand, allowing you to reconcile discrepancies. Regular inventory counts are crucial for effective inventory management, identifying potential loss or theft, and tracking assets for tax purposes. We use a blind count system, meaning current inventory levels are not visible during the count; users will only record the items they physically find. The inventory process is divided into three phases:

  • Counting Status - Items assigned for counting.

  • Counted Status - Items after the initial count has been saved and submitted.

  • Reconciled Status: Counts that have been finalized; variances have been recorded and inventory adjusted.
