Customer Profiles allow stores to collect customer data for sales, repairs, and marketing communications. From a profile, users can create new tickets, view device repair history, manage credit balances, and even schedule or edit existing appointments.

Adding a Customer to RepairQ

On the Navigation Bar, go to Customers → + Add Customer. Alternatively, while on the Customer Overview page, you can click the Add (+) button on the right side of the page.

A Create Customer pop-up window will appear. At a minimum, RepairQ requires the following fields for creating a customer: First Name, Last Name, and Primary Phone. Once satisfied with adding required and optional information, click the + Create button to finalize the process.

Note: If you are on RepairQ Advanced or RepairQ Professional, Admins can customize the required fields by updating Validation Rules for Customers. Any additional fields marked as required will appear on the General customer information tab.

Communication Preferences

Customize your customer’s notification preferences for Service Email/SMS notifications and Marketing Email/SMS notifications. The customer’s SMS Phone, Carrier, and Email information must be filled out for successful communication.

Note: Default selections for these preferences are managed in your Location Information details.

  • Receive Service Email Notifications - Authorize communication for Ticket Approvals and Automated Notifications.

  • Receive Email Marketing - When “Yes,” the customer’s email information is visible in the Marketing Report.

  • Receive Service SMS Notifications - Authorize communication for Ticket Approvals and Automated Notifications.

  • Receive SMS marketing - When “Yes,” the customer’s SMS information is visible in the Marketing Report.

Secondary Information

On the left column of the window, there are category tabs for entering secondary information for the customer.

Note: If additional required fields to create a Customer Profile have been added, these are marked with an asterisk (*) next to their title.

  • Address - Customer address information: City, State, Country, etc.

  • Pricing - Loyalty Card information, automatically applied discounts, Store Credit, and Special Pricing & Tax Terms.

  • Billing - Net Term Billing (Invoicing) and Billing Agent information.

  • Misc - Company Information, Referral Methods, Segments, Preferred Language, and Open Ticket notifications.

Customer Profiles

After creating the customer, you will be taken to their Customer Profile page, where you can view an overview of their details. When viewing this page, the left column has additional management categories and the right side has options to edit their profile and a quick-create button for starting a new ticket with the customer's information attached to it.

Duplicate Customers

When you submit customer details, RepairQ will search your existing customers for a matching entry based on last name and phone number. If a duplicate profile exists, you will be notified with a message with a link to view the existing profile.
