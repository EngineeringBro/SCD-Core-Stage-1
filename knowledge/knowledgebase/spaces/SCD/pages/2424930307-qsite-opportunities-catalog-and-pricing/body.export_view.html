Qsite Opportunities is a RepairQ companion app that engages customers with your store. Opportunities allow customers to search for store locations near them, look up your services and prices, build quotes, and book appointments. When appointments are booked, quote tickets are created in RepairQ with that customer's device and information filled out. Quickly pull up and start tickets, saving you and the customer time. Qsite Opportunities are available to our Advanced and Professional users.

Note: By default, new catalog items will default to Qsite-advertised, however, if this option has been turned off or you offer special pricing for online, there are some settings you can update for this.

Mark an Item as Available on Qsite and Show/Hide Qsite Pricing

By default, new catalog items will default to Qsite-advertised, however, if this option has been turned off, you can turn it back on.

Qsite Visibility

This setting is under the Behaviors

section when editing the Catalog Item details.

  • Advertised - This field defaults to selected when adding and importing new items. For existing items, select this for the item to be shown as an available item/service on Qsite.

Qsite Pricing (Optional)

Online pricing may differ from in-store prices for various reasons, including promotional strategies, cost savings from online operations, targeted marketing efforts, and encouraging online sales.

These settings are located under the Pricing section of the Catalog Item details.

  • Price Advertised - Select whether to show or hide the item’s Price on Qsite.

    • When selected, the price displayed on Qsite will reflect the price entered in the “Advertised Price” field. If that field is blank, the “Price” field will display instead.

  • Advertised Price - By default, the “Price” field is used for standard retail pricing. An alternate price can be added if the price varies when advertised on Qsite. If the “Price Advertised” box is checked, but the “Advertised Price” field is blank, the Price will be displayed instead.
