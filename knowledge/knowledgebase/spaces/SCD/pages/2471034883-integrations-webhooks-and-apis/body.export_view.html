What are Webhooks?

Webhooks are a way for different applications to communicate with each other in real-time. They allow one system to send data to another when specific events occur, such as adding a new customer. This automation can save time and ensure your customer lists are always up-to-date.

What are APIs?

APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) are sets of rules and protocols that allow different software applications to communicate with each other. APIs enable your business software to seamlessly connect with other applications, such as CRM systems, accounting software, and marketing platforms like Mailchimp. This integration ensures data consistency and streamlines operations across different tools.
