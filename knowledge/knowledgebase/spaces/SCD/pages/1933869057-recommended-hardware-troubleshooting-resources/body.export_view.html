RepairQ is a cloud-based system, so we are compatible with many brands of printers, barcode scanners, and other peripherals for your business setup. Our program works best in Chrome Brower, and we also support Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, and Safari browsers.

For a list of our recommended hardware, check out this link: RepairQ Hardware Recommendations.

Our Support Team can assist with any RepairQ-related connectivity issues, however, there may be initial setup procedures for establishing a connection between your device(s) and your computer that are handled independently. Often, there are programs and drivers to install before your device(s) will properly work.
