Invoicing gives your company tools to manage customers and corporate clients with net terms. Invoicing is available to users on our Advanced and Professional plans.

In order to invoice a ticket, it must have either a customer with billing terms enabled or a billing agent attached to the ticket. The ticket must be progressed to Waiting for Payment status before it can be invoiced. After creating an invoice, there may be times when you need to apply a discount to the ticket, write off the ticket, or remove the ticket from the invoice altogether. The tutorials below will guide you through different scenarios.

Making Edits on Invoices

Users can make changes to invoices by opening the Remittance page for the invoice. You can select the Remittance option from the Invoice list or by clicking the Remittance button when viewing an invoice.

Apply a Discount to a Ticket on an Invoice

Under the Invoice Items section of the remittance page, you can apply a discount to a ticket. Enter the discount amount under the Discount column. The tax amount will refactor after the discount is applied. After the discount has been applied, click Save Remittance to submit your change. The discount will reflect on the line item of the invoice.

Remove a Ticket From an Invoice

Under the Invoice Items section of the remittance page, you can click the Remove button to unlink the ticket from the invoice. Removing a ticket will remove pending payments. If a payment has already been posted for a ticket via the invoice, the remove option will not be available. Click Save Remittance to update your change(s). 

Note: Once a ticket has been removed and the invoice has been saved, it cannot be readded but will still be visible if sorting the invoice items list for removed tickets.

Add a Removed Ticket Back to an Invoice

If you have removed a ticket in error and have not saved your changes, you can add it back to the invoice. Click the Add button next to the invoice to add the ticket back to the invoice.

Write Off a Ticket on an Invoice

Writing off a ticket will add a discount for the remaining balance of the ticket and recalculate the new tax amount so the ticket balance due reaches zero. Click Save Remittance to update your change(s) and reconcile the invoice. Discount history for these tickets can be found in the Discounts Detail Report.
