Release 12/17/2018 11:30PM CT (05:30 UTC)

MobileSentrix Purchasing Integration

Now you can order from MobileSentrix directly through your RepairQ purchase orders! RepairQ gets real-time pricing and inventory availability while you build your purchase order, and you can pay for orders directly from RepairQ with a saved credit card or pre-negotiated net terms. Purchase costs are saved for you, ensuring your Cost of Goods Sold is as accurate as possible. MobileSentrix SKUs are saved in your catalog when ordering, so their barcodes are scannable or searchable when selling or counting your inventory. Save yourself hours spent ordering and managing inventory from MobileSentrix when you use the purchasing integration!

Wholesale Gadget Parts Purchasing Integration

Now you can order from Wholesale Gadget Parts directly through your RepairQ purchase orders! RepairQ gets real-time pricing and inventory availability while you build your purchase order, and you can pay for orders on the Wholesale Gadget Parts website. Real-time order updates are received from Wholesale Gadget Parts as your order is processed through picking, packing, and shipping. RepairQ will automatically update the quantity, cost, taxes, shipping cost, and shipment status for you, ensuring your Cost of Goods Sold is as accurate as possible. Wholesale Gadget Parts SKUs are saved in your catalog when ordering, so their barcodes are scannable or searchable when selling or counting your inventory. Save yourself hours spent ordering and managing inventory from Wholesale Gadget Parts when you use the purchasing integration!

Wholesale Gadget Parts RMA Integration

Take the pain and uncertainty out of returns with the integrated RMA system in RepairQ. Using the RMA Tracking system in RepairQ, you can pick, pack, validate, ship, and reconcile your RMA Inventory with the Wholesale Gadget Parts RMA Integration. With the integration you will be validate your RMA batch before shipping to Wholesale Gadget Parts to ensure all of the items will be accepted for processing. When you have a valid batch, you can print a shipping label provided for you by Wholesale Gadget Parts. When Wholesale Gadget Parts process your RMA batch, updates are made in real-time back to your RMA Batch in RepairQ including acceptance and credit amounts for each individual item. With the integration your RMA items can be reconciled for you!

Heartland Integrated Payments

You can now process payments through Heartland Payment Systems! Their PAX S300 payment terminal supports EMV, Swipe, NFC, and Mobile Payments. RepairQ also supports one-click refunds for card-not-present refunds. Credit card payments made on your RepairQ VCT will be auto-reconciled on your Cash Reconciliation Sheet using the Heartland APIs.

Bluefin Integrated Payments

We added a new WiFi capable payment terminal, the PAX D210, to the Bluefin integration. The PAX D210 can process payments wirelessly on a battery, communicating over WiFi network with your point of sale terminal. The PAX D210 also supports the same payment options as the PAX S300 and PAX S500 devices (EMV, Swipe, NFC, and Mobile Payments).

RMA Tracking Updates

Along with the Wholesale Gadget Parts RMA Integration, we have added a few new features to the RMA tracking system in RepairQ. You will be able to use a new RMA Batch status, and you can track new information including supplier RMA #, shipped date, shipper, and tracking number. The User Interface has received a few updates, and you now have the ability to remove items from a batch that is in the “new” status.
