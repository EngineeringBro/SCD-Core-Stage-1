Invoicing gives your company tools to manage customers and corporate clients with net terms. Invoicing is available to users on our Advanced and Professional plans.

Benefits of Invoicing

  • Keep track of corporate relationships — repairs, warranties, and payment terms.

  • Negotiate net terms and discounts with corporate clients and apply those terms and discounts to issued invoices.

  • Expedite corporate transactions by tying customers to accounts and issuing invoices. 

  • Manage Accounts Receivable to ensure on-time payments.

  • View the balance of all tickets associated with an invoice and record payments of several tickets at once.

  • Manage discounts on corporate accounts, ensuring that corporate clients are getting the proper preferred rate for each ticket.
