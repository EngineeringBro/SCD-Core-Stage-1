In RepairQ, carriers are used for classifying devices in the catalog and facilitating SMS notifications. By assigning a carrier to a mobile number, you ensure that communications are properly routed, enhancing customer engagement and support. Carriers can be assigned when creating new Inventory Items, generating a Repair Ticket, and creating a Customer Profile. This classification not only streamlines the management of devices but also ensures accurate tracking and effective communication throughout the repair process.

Note: When adding a new carrier, it is important to include the SMS suffix provided by the mobile carrier. This suffix is crucial for correctly formatting SMS messages and ensuring they reach the intended recipients.

Carrier List

To add a new device carrier, log in as an Admin user. From the Navigation Bar, navigate to Settings → Company Settings. On the Settings Overview page, under the Catalog section, click Carriers.

The Carrier Overview page displays a list of system default carriers along with any carriers created by staff. To create a new carrier, click Add Carrier.

Begin by filling out the carrier fields. Once you are satisfied with your entries, make sure to click Save to apply your changes.

  • Name - The name of the mobile carrier. This identifies the carrier in the system and helps users easily recognize and select the appropriate carrier for devices.

  • SMS Suffix - The carrier gateway is used for SMS delivery (e.g., txt.att.net, vtext.com). This suffix is essential for correctly formatting SMS messages, ensuring they are routed to the intended recipient's mobile network.

  • URL - The website or URL for the mobile carrier. This provides quick access to the carrier’s online resources, such as support or service information, for easy reference.

  • Note - This field allows users to add relevant information that may assist others in understanding the carrier's specific features or requirements.

  • Is Active - When this option is enabled, the carrier will be available in all relevant dropdowns and lists. If it is set to inactive, the carrier will still appear in the list of carriers but will be hidden from view in dropdowns.

After saving, the new carrier will be added to the list. Once a Carrier has been assigned to an Inventory Item, Notification, or any other field, it cannot be deleted. However, if the carrier has not been assigned, it can be deleted by clicking the Red X button next to the carrier name.
