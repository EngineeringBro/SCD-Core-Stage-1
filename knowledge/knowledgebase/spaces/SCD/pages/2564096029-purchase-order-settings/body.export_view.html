Purchase Orders (POs) are a fundamental part of the procurement process, enabling users to receive and document the products they purchase from external suppliers. Integrated with our reporting and inventory management features, Purchase Orders help track spending on ordered products and parts, and maintain accurate inventory levels. Users on our Advanced and Professional plans, with multiple locations or dedicated warehouses, can also facilitate store-to-store inventory transfers using Internal Purchase Orders.

The Purchase Order settings page allows users to customize various aspects of their PO workflow. Users can rename Purchase Order status labels to better fit their process without altering the order of operations. Additionally, the settings provide options to control behaviors for receiving and reconciliation, item conditions, and Internal Transfer shipping costs.

Purchase Order Settings

PO settings are managed at the Company Level (affecting all locations) or the Location Level (Location Override).

On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Company/Location Settings. On the Settings Overview page, under the Catalog category, click Purchase Orders.

Purchase Order Status Labels

Customizing Purchase Order status labels allows your team to align the terminology used within the software with your organization's specific processes and terminology. This can improve clarity and reduce confusion by using familiar terms understood across departments.

Purchase Order General Settings

Customize Purchase Order behaviors and controls for receiving and reconciliation, item conditions, and Internal Transfer shipping costs.

Allow receiving more than the ordered quantity - When enabled, users can record a receipt of quantities greater than what was ordered. When disabled, you cannot receive more than the ordered amount.

Prompt to Reconcile after receiving items - When active, the user is automatically prompted to reconcile the PO after receiving all items on the order.

Only allow the Shipper to edit the shipping cost on internal Purchase Orders - When active, the Shipping Cost field is locked for editing when creating an Internal PO Transfers, however, the shipping location can edit the cost when fulfilling the order.

Allow Item Condition Specification in Purchase Orders - When active, users can set Item Conditions during purchase order creation, rather than during the receiving process. This information will be stored for the order when receiving items.
