The Help Desk is designed to enhance your support experience by offering self-help options through our Knowledge Base and allowing users to submit and manage support requests.

Creating a User Account will allow you to create a support request, review ticket progress, and view a history of any issues submitted to the Support Team.

Note: If you are corresponding via email, make sure to update your Approved Senders List with the following email addresses to ensure you receive our response: no-reply@automation.atlassian.com, jira@automation.atlassian.com, and jira@servicecentral.atlassian.net. If you have not received a response in a timely manner, we also recommend checking your spam and trash folders before sending a follow-up to our team.

Create a User Account

1. On your computer or mobile device, click this link to view our Support Knowledge Base. When viewing the landing page, click Log in.

2. On the Help Center login page, click Sign Up.

3. On the Sign Up page, enter your email address and click Send Link. You will receive an email with a link to finish the signup process.

4. After you receive the email, click the Sign up link to very your email address. Enter your name and create a password. After you field out the fields, click Sign up to complete the process.

5. You will be directed to the login screen. Enter your email address and click Next. Then, enter your password and click Log in. You will be directed to the main page of the Help Desk.

Updating Your Profile Details

When viewing the Help Desk homepage, click the Profile Icon on the upper-right of the page. From the list of options, click Profile.

You can update your personal details, change your password, and update your preferred language and timezone. After updating your info, be sure to Save your settings.
