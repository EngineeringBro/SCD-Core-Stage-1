Employees can use the clock in/out feature to accurately record their work hours, enabling managers to monitor hours worked during pay periods, track absenteeism, and maintain precise profitability reports. Staff members with the "Timesheet Required" option enabled in their Staff Profile can clock in/out directly within RepairQ. Employees can clock in/out from any open terminal without logging in under their profile.

Note: Administrators can customize the required credentials for clocking in/out. For details, refer to our article, Staff Management Settings.

Clocking In (Starting the Timecard)

On the top Navigation Bar, click the Location Dropdown. From the list of selections, click Clock In/Out. A Time Clock pop-up will appear. Enter your Username and/or PIN/Password and click Go.

Note: Admins can update authentication username/pin requirements for clocking in/out in Staff Management Settings.

The pop-up will show your current Time Clock Status and the total hours for today and the current week. Click Clock In to begin recording your time.

You will be asked to confirm that you would like to clock in. Click Confirm, and you will be notified that your clock-in was successful and the time clock has started. You can also see your clock-in status on the dashboard under the Get Started section.

Clock Out (Stop the Timecard)

Navigate to Location Dropdown → Clock In/Out and enter your login PIN/Password. The pop-up will show your current Time Clock Status and the total logged hours for today and the current week. Click Clock Out to stop recording your time. You are asked to confirm that you would like to clock out. Click Confirm, and you will be notified that your clock-out was successful and the time clock has stopped.
