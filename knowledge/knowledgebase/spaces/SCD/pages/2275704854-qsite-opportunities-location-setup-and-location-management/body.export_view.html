Qsite is a RepairQ companion app that provides ways for your customers to engage with your store. There are two tiers of Qsite: Qsite Ticket Status and Qsite Opportunities.

Qsite Ticket Status provides an easy way for your customers to check their ticket status, ticket notes, pending balance, and store contact information. This feature is available to all users, regardless of subscription plan.

Qsite Opportunities allows customers to search for store locations near them, look up your services and prices, build quotes, and book appointments. Ticket approvals can also be managed via Qsite. This feature is available to our Advanced and Professional users.

Add a Location in Qsite Opportunities Location Search Results

For Qsite Opportunities to successfully locate your location in search results, you will need to update the coordinates in your Location Info.

1. Log into the appropriate location. On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Location Settings. On the Settings page, click Location Info.

2. Scroll down until you see the Latitude and Longitude fields. Fill out this information and Save your settings.

Qsite Search Settings and Location Management

On the Navigation Bar, go to Settings → Company Settings. On the Settings page, look for the Qsite section and click Locations.

Location Search Settings

Search settings allow you to configure your search range (in miles or kilometers), set the total of returned locations in search results, and customize the title and description of your Location Search page.

Manage Active Locations

The Location List provides location details (based on the corresponding location settings) with options to enable/disable a location from showing in location search results.
