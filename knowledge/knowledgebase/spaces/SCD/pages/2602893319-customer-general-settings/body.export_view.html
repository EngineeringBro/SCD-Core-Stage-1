Customer Profiles are an essential feature of RepairQ, allowing users to link Ticket history to individual profiles. This capability allows for efficient tracking of customer purchases, device repairs, and trade-ins, making it easy to access records for returns, warranty claims, and more.

The Customer General Settings page allows you to customize how customer information is managed and accessed. These settings enable you to control search algorithms, validate zip codes, manage location-based search permissions, restrict access to customer quotes, and handle third-party marketing consents. By configuring these settings, you can optimize customer data management to fit your business needs and ensure efficient and secure handling of customer profiles.

Customer General Settings

To find Customer Settings, navigate to Company Settings → Customers → General Settings.

General Settings

This page allows you to configure settings for search behaviors, zip code validation, sharing Customer List data, and marketing preferences.

  • Enable Legacy Customer Search - When active, our search algorithm will split keyword phrases into separate search terms.

  • Limit the Ticket Customer Search to the locations the user has access to

    • Inactive - When linking a customer to a ticket, search results include profiles from all locations on the database.

    • Active - When linking a customer to a ticket, search results are limited to the locations where the user has a role assignment.

  • Validate Zip Code for US, CA, AU, and GB countries - When a user creates or edits a Customer Profile, RepairQ will perform a validation check to ensure the zip code is in the correct format for the assigned country.

    • RepairQ will validate the zip code for the correct country, but will not validate for the specific city/state/region.

    • Zip code validation will override any Validation Rules set for the zip code field. When active, a zip code will always be required for customer profiles.

  • Do Not Share Customer Lists Between Locations - This restricts staff to searching profiles from locations where they have a role assignment.

    • When active, this removes the “Search All Locations” filter from the Customer List.

    • When active, only Admins can search for Customer Profiles from deactivated (closed) locations.

  • Restrict Non-Admin Staff from Viewing Customer Leads in Disallowed Locations - When active, non-admin staff can only view Quotes from locations where they have a role assignment.

Third-Party Marketing

Users on our Advanced and Professional plans can obtain marketing consent from customers, and share marketing data with a third party. Admins can customize the form details, and users can update customer consent at any time by editing the Customer Profile or when a customer is added to a ticket.

Consent form content can be found in Analytics Reports, and, by using the automated report feature, this information can be sent to the third party directly.

For more information on this feature, check out Third-Party Marketing Consent.
