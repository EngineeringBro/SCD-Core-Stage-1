Users with a Sales role or higher can transfer tickets between locations where they have an assigned role. Additionally, Admins can manually update the location by adjusting the Ticket Properties. However, unlike the standard transfer process, this manual update does not automatically create a System Log entry for the transfer.

Transfer Tickets Between Locations

On the Navigation Bar, go to Tickets → Ticket Queue. On the Tickets page, use the filters to locate the ticket you need to transfer. Click the Options button under the Actions column next to the entry. From the list of options, click Transfer.

The Ticket Transfer modal appears, and the user can select the ticket transfer destination. Optionally, select an assignee for the ticket and add any notes. When you are satisfied with your selections, click Transfer Ticket.

After submitting the transfer, you are taken to the Ticket Edit screen for the ticket you transferred. At this point, you can Save the ticket. RepairQ will automatically note the ticket and mark it as important. This allows staff to see that the ticket was transferred. On the Ticket View screen, you will see the ticket's Current Location (house icon) and the Created Location (drop pin icon).
