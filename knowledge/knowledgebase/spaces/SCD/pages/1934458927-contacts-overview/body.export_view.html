A Contact (lead) is classified as a potential customer for your business. Contact Profiles can be created to collect customer data used for sales, repairs, and marketing communication. When appointments are booked on Qsite, a Contact Profile is also created in RepairQ. Users can easily create a quote from a profile that can be printed, emailed, and converted into tickets after they are accepted. 

A Contact Profile has all of the data points of a standard Customer Profile with a couple of key differences:

  • Contacts can be viewed separately on the Contacts page.

  • If a ticket with an assigned Contact is completed, the contact is automatically converted to a Customer.

Searching for Existing Contacts

Users can quick-search for a specific Contact or use the Navigation Bar to go to Opportunities → Contacts to view the Contact List and filter for results.

Converting an Existing Contact Profile to a Customer Profile

A Contact Profile will automatically convert to a Customer Profile if a ticket with that contact assigned is completed. Users can also update this information without creating a ticket. When editing a Contact Profile, go to the Misc tab on the Edit Contact pop-up.

On the Miscellaneous Tab, uncheck the box next to "Not a customer yet?" and Save your changes.

When viewing the Contact List page, the Customer column will indicate whether this entry is classified as a Contact or has been converted to a Customer.
