Updating Your Connection Settings in Windows 11

1. On your computer, navigate to Settings.

2. On the Settings page, click Network & Internet on the left column.

3. On the Network & internet status screen, next to your active connection, click Properties.

4. On the active network screen, look for the DNS Server Assignment selection. Click the Edit button next to this option to open the settings.

5. On the Edit network DNS settings popup, update the following fields. When finished with your changes, click Save.

  • Automatic (DHCP) or Manual - Manual

  • IPv4 - ON

  • Preferred DNS - 8.8.8.8

  • DNS over HTTPS - OFF

  • Alternate DNS - 8.8.4.4

  • DNS over HTTPS - OFF

  • IPv6 - OFF

6. When viewing your active network settings, you can see the updated DNS settings.
